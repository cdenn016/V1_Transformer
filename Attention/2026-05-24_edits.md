# 2026-05-24 — GL(K) Attention manuscript pair: peer-review revisions

Edits applied to address findings in the peer review at
`Attention/REVIEW_GLK_2026-05-24/peer_review.md` (5 major items, ~10 minor items).
Both `GL(K)_attention.tex` (main) and `GL(K)_supplementary.tex` (supplementary)
were modified. Both files compile cleanly to PDF.

## Major revisions

### M1 — Conditional Uniqueness of forward KL (Supplementary Appendix H)

- **M1.1 Step 3 proof gap (supp:1269–1290).** Inserted the missing specialization
  step. The proof now subtracts the rearranged target from the general
  stationarity condition to obtain a β-weighted sum residual
  (`eq:residual_sum_supp`), then specializes to a configuration where all
  transported neighbors coincide pointwise with a common reference density, so
  that `Σβ = 1` collapses the sum to the single-ratio identity
  `f'(r) − log r = k`. The real-analyticity argument then extends to all of
  `(0,∞)`.
- **M1.2 Drop unused hypothesis (supp:1096).** Removed assumption (iii)
  "exponential-family closure" — it never enters the proof; it is a consequence
  of `f'(t) = log t`, demoted to a remark.
- **M1.3 χ² intuition (supp:1090).** Rewrote the χ² sentence: the χ² stationarity
  is transcendental (it mixes `q_i` and `log q_i`), not polynomial-ratio. The
  theorem's exclusion of χ² is unaffected and the underlying reason is now
  correctly stated.
- **M1.4 Rényi extension (main:1062, 1074).** Qualified: only the softmax form
  of β extends to Rényi `D_α`; the geometric-mean closed form for `q*` is
  KL-specific and the Rényi stationarity for `q*` is transcendental. Added an
  explicit pointer to Appendix H.

### M2 — RG section status and language (Main §6.4, Supplementary Appendix F)

- **M2.1 Downgrade Theorem → Proposition (main:2258, supp:956).** Renamed
  `\begin{theorem}` to `\begin{proposition}`; all `Theorem~\ref{thm:rg_conditional}`
  references in main and supp updated to `Proposition~\ref{thm:rg_conditional}`.
  Added a sentence stating the Proposition is direct CLT applied to the
  definitions in Eqs.~(g1)–(g3) and that no gauge content enters the calculation.
- **M2.2 Edge-contraction rate prose (supp:949).** Rewrote: edges contract as
  `n^{-1}` (not `n^{-1/2}`) under the bilinear average
  `Ω_{AB} = (|A||B|)^{-1} Σ Ω_{ij}` defined at supp:940 (averages `n²` edges by
  CLT). Linear-order holonomy expansion then gives `||H−I|| ∼ n^{-1}` ⇒
  `y_3 = −1`, matching both the matrix and the synthetic table.
- **M2.3 Validation language (supp:988).** Replaced "the results confirm the
  predicted exponents to three significant figures" with explicit framing as a
  sanity check that the analytic CLT reproduces on synthetic i.i.d. data — not
  validation of any framework claim. Mirror update at main:2280.
- **M2.4 H1/H2 alignment (supp:1026).** Replaced the unconditional `N → ∞`
  extrapolation with the two-hypothesis (H1 finite-size bias / H2 genuine
  correlation) framing from main:2280, including the crossover-vs-correction
  caveat.
- **M2.5 K² → √K (main:2275, supp:956, supp:968).** Conjecture clause (b) now
  reads "heuristic O(√K)-class advantage … the exponent is a class label, not a
  derived bound" and notes that no quantitative sample-complexity bound (VC,
  PAC-Bayes, Chinchilla) underwrites the √K rate.

### M3 — Multi-head identification (Main §4.1.3, Table 1, §4.6)

- **M3a Subspace disclosure (main:1257).** Added a new "Scope of the head-space
  identification" paragraph after the thin-SVD lift, stating that the framework
  derives the per-head invertible bilinear `M_h^a` and value aggregation up to
  `W_O` absorption, but does **not** derive the rectangular subspace embeddings
  `U_Q^a, U_K^a, U_V^a` — these are structural design choices orthogonal to the
  gauge derivation.
- **M3b Table 1 row downgrades (main:1662, 1692, 1700).** Multi-head head-space
  kernel row reclassified from D to D♯; added new row for rectangular subspace
  embeddings marked S. Boltzmann GLU vs GELU/SiLU row changed from S to I.
  Caption now defines I = "interpretive correspondence: same family,
  not functionally identical" (e.g., Gaussian PDF vs CDF).

### M4 — Gauge frame gradient convention (Supplementary Appendices C, D)

- **M4a Declarative convention paragraph (supp:397).** Added a new
  "Convention" paragraph at the head of Appendix C declaring that
  `∂F/∂φ^a` is defined via the right-trivialised differential
  `D_φ(exp)[T_a] = Q_a^R · exp(φ)` throughout, computed by autograd through
  `torch.matrix_exp`. The Cartan-modified preconditioner `g̃_{ab}` of
  Section~\ref{sec:glk_preconditioning} acts on these components.
- **M4b Reconcile App D with App C (supp:655).** Replaced the left-trivialised
  re-derivation with a clear cross-reference to the right-trivialised
  declaration in App C. The left-trivialised form is displayed for didactic
  comparison; the implementation uses the right-trivialised form throughout.
  Removed the contradiction with supp:486 (the "K > 3 symmetric quadrature"
  claim) — for general K, the right-trivialised generator is recovered from
  PyTorch's Fréchet derivative as `Q_a^R = D_φ(exp)[T_a]·e^{-φ}`.

### M5 — Bundle scaffolding leaks (Main §3, §4.6)

- **M5a Notation table footnote (main:400).** Added inline description on
  `Φ_i, Φ̃_i` clarifying they are introduced in Supplementary Appendix A for
  cross-reference with the companion paper and are not used in any equation of
  this paper.
- **M5b Regime II conditional (main:1742).** Opened the per-head holonomy
  paragraph with an explicit Regime II conditional: "the vertex-frame
  parameterization adopted throughout the main paper places the model in
  Regime I, where the holonomy vanishes identically … the companion paper
  develops the edge-relaxed Regime II construction in detail; under that
  extension …". The subsequent block-diagonal factorization is now stated
  conditionally rather than as a fact of the main paper's model.
