## Major revisions

### M1 — Conditional Uniqueness of forward KL (Supplementary Appendix H)

- **M1.1 Step 3 proof gap (supp:1269–1290).** Inserted the missing specialization
  step. The proof now subtracts the rearranged target from the general
  stationarity condition to obtain a β-weighted sum residual
  (`eq:residual_sum_supp`), then specializes to a configuration where all
  transported neighbors coincide pointwise with a common reference density, so
  that `Σβ = 1` collapses the sum to the single-ratio identity
  `f'(r) − log r = k`. The real-analyticity argument then extends to all of
  `(0,∞)`.
- **M1.2 Drop unused hypothesis (supp:1096).** Removed assumption (iii)
  "exponential-family closure" — it never enters the proof; it is a consequence
  of `f'(t) = log t`, demoted to a remark.
- **M1.3 χ² intuition (supp:1090).** Rewrote the χ² sentence: the χ² stationarity
  is transcendental (it mixes `q_i` and `log q_i`), not polynomial-ratio. The
  theorem's exclusion of χ² is unaffected and the underlying reason is now
  correctly stated.
- **M1.4 Rényi extension (main:1062, 1074).** Qualified: only the softmax form
  of β extends to Rényi `D_α`; the geometric-mean closed form for `q*` is
  KL-specific and the Rényi stationarity for `q*` is transcendental. Added an
  explicit pointer to Appendix H.

### M2 — RG section status and language (Main §6.4, Supplementary Appendix F)

  `y_3 = −1`, matching both the matrix and the synthetic table.
- **M2.3 Validation language (supp:988).** Replaced "the results confirm the
  predicted exponents to three significant figures" with explicit framing as a

- **M2.4 H1/H2 alignment (supp:1026).** Replaced the unconditional `N → ∞`

## A. SO(3)/SO(K) → GL⁺(K) reframe

- **Title + intro.** Section retitled to GL(K); intro now states GL⁺(K) transport
  (the connected component reached by `Ω_ij = exp(φ_i)exp(−φ_j)`, `eq:transport_def`),
  with SO(K) as the skew-frame special case. Consistent with the body group
  `Ω_ij ∈ GL⁺(K)` (`:1289`), `GL(d_head)^H ⊂ GL(K)` (`:1352`), and the code default
  `gauge_group='GLK'` (`transformer/vfe/config.py:149`).
- **Gauge-covariance transformation laws (correctness fix).** The old block stated,
  for `g ∈ SO(3)`, `∇_Σ S ↦ g(∇_Σ S)g^T`. That law is **wrong for non-orthogonal g**.
  Under the GL(K) congruence `Σ_i ↦ gΣ_ig^T` the Euclidean gradient transforms
  contravariantly, `∇_Σ S ↦ g^{−T}(∇_Σ S)g^{−1}`, while only the natural gradient
  `2Σ(∇S)Σ` stays covariant, `↦ g(·)g^T`; the two coincide iff g is orthogonal
  (the SO(K) case), which is why the SO(3) framing hid the error. Numerically
  confirmed (K=3, det g = −4.45): contravariant residual 8e-17, covariant residual
  6e-14, old SO-law residual 0.24. Added the affine-invariant-metric uniqueness
  statement with `\cite{Pennec2006, Amari2016, Bhatia2007}`.
- **Term 1 / direct KL gradient (correctness fix).** The general GL⁺(K) form is now
  primary: the gradient lives in the full `gl(K)` (no antisymmetrization) and the
  `log det` piece is retained because `Ω^{−T} ≠ Ω`. The stale claim that "the
  manuscript's simulations use [the SO(K) antisymmetric] restriction" was removed
  (code default is GLK). SO(K) demoted to the skew special case where `log det`
  vanishes. **The existing `eq:kl_omega_directional` multline was numerically wrong**
  (FD 16.15 vs the multline's 377.78) and was replaced by the verified form
  `∂_X KL = tr[X^T(I − ΛΣ_i − Λr(Ω_ij μ_j)^T − Λr r^T)]`, `Λ=A^{-1}`, `A=Ω_ijΣ_jΩ_ij^T`,
  `r=μ_i−Ω_ijμ_j` (independent FD residual ~1e-9 in both K=3 and K=4 checks; the
  quadratic term uses `ΛA=I`). `eq:kl_omega_unconstrained` kept as the hedged
  dominant-contribution form, now naming the omitted quadratic `−Λr r^TΛΩΣ_j`.

## B. State-dependent precision α_i (eq 240 + product rule)

# 2026-05-24 — Participatory_it_from_bit.tex: sender contributions to the gradient appendix

`∇_{Σ_i}S`, `∇_{φ_i}S` were titled "(Complete)" but were receiver-only — they
differentiated only `S_belief = Σ_j β_ij KL(q_i‖Ω_ij q_j)` (pairs where i attends).
Agent i also enters S as a sender in pairs (ℓ,i): `Σ_ℓ β_ℓi KL(q_ℓ‖Ω_ℓi q_i)`. The
body already requires both sums (`eq:envelope_gradient`) and already derives the
complete mean gradient (`eq:total_gradient_mu`) and sender covariance gradient
(line 1962). Added the missing sender contribution to the appendix. Single file:
`Attention/Participatory_it_from_bit.tex`. Two investigator agents (gauge-theorist,
info-geometer) + verifier (vfe-manuscript-reviewer); every gradient FD-verified.

  belief covariances, gauge frames).
- New subsection "Sender Contributions and the Total Gradient" before "Complete Update
  Equations":

    `∂E_ℓi/∂μ_i = Λ_{q_i}Ω_ℓi^{-1}(Ω_ℓi μ_i − μ_ℓ)` and
