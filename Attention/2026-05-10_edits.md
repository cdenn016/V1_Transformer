# 2026-05-10 Edits — Pullback Construction: Move Perceived Geometry from Prior `p_i` to Generative Model `s_i`

## Summary

Reframed the manuscript's central pullback claim. The previous framing identified "the agent's perceived geometry of reality" with the prior pullback `G^(p)`. After parallel investigation, the correct identification is with the generative-model pullback `G^(s)` on the model fiber `B_model`, with `G^(r)` (hyper-prior) carrying the strictly innate part of perceived structure, and `G^(p)`, `G^(q)` recast as expectational and epistemic overlays on the slower structural background.

## Motivation

Five independent lines of argument converge on the same answer.

1. Vacher, Briand, Mamassian, Wichmann (ICLR 2024, arXiv:2310.11759) showed empirically that human perceptual scales for naturalistic textures track the Fisher information of the generative model of the stimuli, not of any momentary posterior.
2. Hierarchical active inference (Friston 2017 deep temporal models) treats Bayesian parameter learning of the generative model as the source of structural perceptual organisation, distinct from Bayesian inference at the state level.
3. Hoffman's interface theory of perception treats perceived space as an evolved, slowly-changing interface — model-fiber, not state-fiber.
4. Kant's transcendental aesthetic treats space as a structural a priori "form of intuition," which maps onto slow, learned/innate model parameters rather than onto an instantaneous expectation.
5. The general-relativity analogy already invoked in the manuscript: the metric is a structural background field, not an instantaneous-belief quantity, and the framework's slow-channel `s_i` is the right type-level for that role.

The manuscript's own internal tension corroborates the move:
- Line 2176 (pre-edit) explicitly stated `s_i` does NOT directly shape `p_i`, contradicting the parenthetical at line 2180 that justified `G^(p)` by appeal to `s_i` shaping `p_i`.
- The altered-states passage near line 3220 (pre-edit) already had `s_i` as the cause and `p_i` as derivative carrier of "modified spacetime structure" — exactly the move now made explicit.
