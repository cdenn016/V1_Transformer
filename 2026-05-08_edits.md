## Concern B: Sender covariance gradient at line 1536

The original manuscript form differed by a Frobenius norm of order one off consensus and was identical to the corrected form at consensus.

Definition of `\tilde d_{ik} := \tilde \mu_k - \mu_i = \Omega_{ik}\mu_k - \mu_i` added below the equation, with a paragraph explaining the origin of the contribution via `\partial \tilde\Lambda / \partial \tilde\Sigma = -\tilde\Lambda \otimes \tilde\Lambda` chain-ruled through `\tilde\Sigma_k = \Omega_{ik}\Sigma_k\Omega_{ik}^T`. The choice has downstream consequences for the consensus-detector derivation, the lattice-gauge identities, and the discrete Regime II realization. No D edits were applied in this commit; the user has been informed and asked to direct the resolution.
