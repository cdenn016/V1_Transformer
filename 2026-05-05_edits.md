# 2026-05-05 Edits

## Editing `Attention/Participatory_it_from_bit.tex` after multi-agent peer review

### Why

A four-reviewer + one-verifier peer-review pass on
`Attention/Participatory_it_from_bit.tex` (using `/peer-review` skill,
parallel investigation agents, and an independent verifier) surfaced
six critical issues, fourteen major issues, and dozens of minor ones.
The user identified an additional structural insight during review:
hyper-priors $r$ are not primitive fields but cross-scale shadows of the
meta-agent's generative model $s$, mirroring the existing top-down
prior propagation $p_i^{(s)} = \Omega_{i,I}[q_I^{(s+1)}]$. This collapses
the four-bundle bookkeeping prescribed by Reviewer 1 into a cleaner
two-manifold-per-scale structure, with $p$ and $r$ as derived objects.

The plan was to apply the keystone two-manifold rewrite plus
mechanical, framing, and self-plagiarism fixes in eight phases, scoped
per the user's decisions: (1) all eight phases proceed; (2) Wheeler1990
duplicate dedup chosen by inspecting bib entries against body usage;
(3) critical-exponent language reframed as single-seed; (4) verbatim
self-plagiarism phrases paraphrased; (5) missing citations left as
TODO; (6) keystone applied directly with the planned outline.
