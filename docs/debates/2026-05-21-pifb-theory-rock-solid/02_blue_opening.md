## Falsification conditions

This position is wrong if any one of the following holds.

(a) **Sandwich misuse downstream of line 1897.** The boxed identity $\Omega_{ji}^\top\tilde\Lambda_{q_i}^{(j)}\Omega_{ji}=\Lambda_{q_i}$ at line 1897 is GL-valid; the secondary identities $\tilde\Lambda_k\Omega_{ik}=\Omega_{ik}\Lambda_k$ and $\Omega_{ki}^\top\tilde\Lambda_i=\Lambda_i\Omega_{ki}^\top$ at line 1897 hold only under $\mathrm{O}(d)$. If any §Theory equation past line 1897 uses these O(d)-only identities outside the O(d) limit without re-flagging, sub-claim 1 (mathematical correctness) fails (`02_blue_memo_geometer.md`).

(b) **Surrogate-substitution past line 1404.** The autograd-vs-reduced-free-energy gradient gap is named once at line 1404 with the covariance correction $-\tau^{-1}\mathrm{Cov}_{\beta^*}(E,\nabla_x E)$. If any subsequent §Theory derivation silently substitutes $\nabla\langle E\rangle_\beta$ for $\nabla\mathcal{F}_{\mathrm{red}}$ at the gradient level without re-flagging, sub-claim 1 fails (`02_blue_memo_info-geometer.md`).

(c) **Appendix-A dependency on closure of the f-divergence uniqueness theorem.** The mixture-of-sources construction's selection of forward KL (as opposed to reverse KL, Hellinger, Rényi-α) is justified post-hoc at line 1044 via the conditional uniqueness theorem in Appendix~A (cited at lines 1038, 1044, 1112). If Appendix~A does not actually establish closure under the stated assumptions (i)–(iii), or if assumption (iii) silently smuggles in the forward-KL choice it is meant to derive, the variational defense at sub-claim 1 fails (`02_blue_memo_variational.md`).

(d) **Residual-subgroup discipline violated past line 571.** The blanket statement at line 571 commits to attaching the residual-constant-per-agent-subgroup restriction at every Role-B occurrence. A single later §Theory equation that asserts gauge-invariance without naming the residual restriction would falsify the gauge-theory defense at sub-claim 4 (`02_blue_memo_gauge-theorist.md`).

(e) **Literalist reading of sub-claims 5 and 6 carries the conjunctive logic.** The conjunctive operationalization at §00_claim.md states "any one [sub-claim] failing falsifies the whole." Under the strictest literal reading, sub-claim 5 fails (load-bearing `\cite{Dennis2025trans}` cites at lines 1609, 1617, 1631, 1696, 1875) and sub-claim 6 fails (`\textbf{TODO:}` at line 1880). The blue position holds only under the intent-faithful reading where companion-paper cross-cites cover scope extensions and future-work stubs that name what is *not* claimed are publication-acceptable. If the chief judge adopts the strict literal reading, blue loses on the operationalization regardless of how sub-claims 1–4 fare (`02_blue_memo_philosophy-of-science.md`).
