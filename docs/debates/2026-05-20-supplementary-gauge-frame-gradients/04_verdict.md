## Action

The corrective action on §C is bounded to a defined edit set, with three substantive mathematical revisions and two editorial unifications.

The first mathematical revision replaces line 599's `d exp_φ(T_a) = exp(φ) · Ψ(ad_φ)(T_a)` with `Ψ(z) = (e^z − 1)/z` by one of the canonical Fréchet-derivative forms. Either the left-trivialized form `D_φ exp[T_a] = exp(φ) · ((1 − e^{−ad_φ})/ad_φ)(T_a)` with generator `(1 − e^{−z})/z`, or the right-trivialized form `D_φ exp[T_a] = ((e^{ad_φ} − I)/ad_φ)(T_a) · exp(φ)` with the original generator placed AFTER `exp(φ)`. The canonical reference is [Hall 2015 §2.7 / Theorem 5.4]. The choice should match whichever convention §C.3's transport derivatives consume — by the right-trivialized identity `D_{φ_i} exp[T_a] = Q_a^{(i)} · exp(φ_i)` at line 541, the right-trivialized form is the consistent choice.

Either drop the `−(1/2)` prefactor at line 587 (so the line-589 "equivalently" claim holds as line 590 is written) or divide line 590 by 2 (so the formula matches the definition).
