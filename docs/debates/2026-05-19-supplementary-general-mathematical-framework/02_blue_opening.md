# Blue Opening — supplementary-general-mathematical-framework

## Steelman (opposing position)

A section titled "General Mathematical Framework" that omits the Fisher-Rao metric, the closed-form Gaussian KL, the natural gradient, the variational free energy functional, and the E-step/M-step decomposition while introducing notation (`p_i ∈ B_p`, line 76) that is inconsistent with the main paper's own §3.1 (where `p_i(k_i) = N(k_i; μ_{0,i}^{(q)}, Σ_{0,i}^{(q)})` lives in `E_q`, lines 602–608) cannot be called "complete and mathematically pure" — the title promises a comprehensive foundational chapter and the four subsections deliver a scaffold whose own load-bearing names (statistical manifold, `B_q`/`B_p`) are introduced but never instrumented with the metric and divergence structure the main paper invokes.

## Position

The supplementary's §General Mathematical Framework (lines 46–177) is complete and mathematically pure *as the bundle-scaffold chapter of an eight-chapter supplementary*. Sub-claims α (bundle scaffold), β (Gaussian-belief state space), and γ (gauge connection and transport) are established inside lines 46–177 and match the canonical forms of [Nakahara2003 Ch. 9–10] and [Frankel2011 Ch. 17]. Sub-claims δ (natural-gradient information geometry) and ε (variational EM machinery) are deferred — *within the same supplementary* — to §B Covariance Dynamics (lines 178–387), §C Gauge Frame Gradients (lines 388–610), and §D Variational Gradient Descent (lines 611–665), and the main paper at `Attention/GL(K)_attention.tex:1453` explicitly forward-references "Supplementary Appendix D for derivation" for exactly the Fisher-Rao natural-gradient material. The deferral is therefore not a gap; it is the supplementary's pedagogical architecture, and it works at runtime: every Fisher-Rao / Gaussian-KL / natural-gradient invocation in the main paper resolves into a later supplementary chapter, not into thin air. The single concrete notational tension — `p_i ∈ B_p` at line 76 versus `p_i(k_i) ∈ E_q` at main-paper line 602 — is a labeling ambiguity at the scaffold level that does not propagate into any derivation in lines 46–177 (no equation in §General Mathematical Framework dereferences `B_p` to obtain `p_i`'s parameters), and is therefore a copy-edit issue, not a load-bearing failure.
