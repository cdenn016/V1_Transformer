1. Whether "mathematical realization of Wheeler's 'it from bit' vision" at 3454 is consistent with the conditional mood applied to 3187. The "might emerge" in the same sentence provides some hedging but "providing mathematical realization" is declarative.
2. Whether "rigorous derivations (transformers from gauge theory)" at 3458 is consistent with 3190's "one interpretive lens among several equally rigorous readings." The 3458 phrasing could refer to: (a) the mathematical derivation of the transformer-recovery (which is rigorous as math), or (b) the gauge-theoretic interpretation of transformers (which the manuscript itself frames as one lens among several). The ambiguity may be load-bearing.
3. Whether "Only transformer connections have thus far been empirically validated" at 3458 is correct scope. The framework's transformer experiments validate certain claims (recovery, holonomy diagnostics, scaling) but "transformer connections as gauge theory" is a stronger claim than "transformer connections work as a model."
