# Red Rebuttal — pifb-spec-3-consensus-dim

## Concession

Three points of blue's opening I grant on the evidence.

First, the Haar disclosure at PIFB:2928 is thorough and correctly anchored. The three obstructions blue lists — constant-g cyclicity-triviality, infinite-dimensional functional integral over `g: C -> G` for local g, and infinite Haar volume on non-compact SO(1,3) — match standard canon. The Maurer-Cartan transformation `A -> g^{-1}Ag + g^{-1}dg` for local gauge transformations is the canonical form [Nakahara2003 §10.3]. The Faddeev-Popov framing for path-integral quantization is the standard fix [PeskinSchroeder1995 §16.2]. The infinite-volume statement for non-compact Lie groups is standard [Folland1995 §2.2; Knapp2002 §VIII.2]. I do not contest the regulator-gap disclosure.

Second, the base-vs-fiber distinction stated at PIFB:2970 — "G_i(c) is an n × n matrix with n := dim(C), and has at most n eigenvalues" — is precisely the pullback-rank statement [Lee2013 §11; Nakahara2003 §5.4]. The 2970 paragraph itself is correct, and the followup at PIFB:2996 ("only dim(C) directions in T_{q_i(c)}B are in the image of d σ_i") is consistent with 2970.

Third, the spectral decomposition at PIFB:2952 is ordinary linear algebra. The pullback `σ_i^* g_B` of a positive-semidefinite symmetric (0,2) tensor is symmetric, so the real spectral theorem applies [HornJohnson2013 Thm 2.5.6].

These three concessions cover the Haar-volume canon vector, the base-vs-fiber statement at 2970, and the spectral-theorem citation. Blue's defense on those vectors holds.

## Core attack

Blue's load-bearing weakness is that it treats blue's clean statement at PIFB:2970 as if it disciplined the rest of the subsection. It does not. Two sentence-level violations land inside the same subsection 2945–3024 that 2970 explicitly warns against, and one of them is the section's own punchline.

**Violation 1 — base/fiber conflation at PIFB:3024 (inside the §Speculative subsection).** The text reads: "Only a tiny sliver (e.g. 4 dimensions out of K) becomes phenomenal spacetime. The remainder is internal structure that never manifests spatially or perceptually." Here `K` is the Gaussian dimension parameter (PIFB:2962, "for K-dimensional Gaussian distributions"; PIFB:2968 instantiates `K = 768`, `dim(B) = K(K+3)/2 = 296,064`). But the eigenvalue spectrum of `G_i(c)` lives on `T_c C` with at most `n = dim(C)` eigenvalues, per 2970. The phrase "4 out of K" therefore conflates a *fiber* parameter `K` (which sets `dim(B)`) with the *base*-tangent dimension `n = dim(C)` that bounds the spectrum. Under the 2970 reading, the correct count is "4 out of n" with `n = dim(C)`, and K appears nowhere in it. This is the canonical confusion the pullback rank theorem disciplines [Lee2013 §11, "rank(σ^* g_B)_c ≤ rank(dσ_c) ≤ min(dim C, dim B)"]. Blue's falsification condition (4) lists exactly this trigger: "any sentence between [2970 and 3016] that uses fiber-dimension language to describe the spectrum of G_i." PIFB:3024 is 54 lines downstream of 2970 in the same subsection (just past the §Speculative break at 3018) and uses fiber-dimension language `K` to describe the spectrum count. The trigger lands.

A weaker reading — that "K" in 3024 is shorthand for the full belief manifold rather than the Gaussian dim — is foreclosed by 2962–2968, which fixes `K` as the Gaussian width parameter and explicitly reports `dim(B) = 296,064` at `K = 768`. The text uses two different scalars in two sentences for the same dimension count.

**Violation 2 — silent inheritance of the signature postulates at PIFB:2980.** Blue claims the §Speculative subsection title at 3018 and the disclaimer at 3022 cover the conditional epistemic status. That defense applies to the *count* (`|D_obs| = 4`) but not to the *signature*. The line at 2980 reads: "For human agents, we conjecture this comprises approximately 4 dimensions (1 temporal + 3 spatial)." The "1 temporal" is not a property the real spectral theorem produces — the eigenvalues `λ_a` of `G_i(c) = σ_i^* g_B` are non-negative by construction (PIFB:2956: `λ_n ≥ 0`), so any temporal direction must come from an *indefinite* form, which in this framework only exists under the GL(K,C) extension plus imaginary-frame postulate plus real-part projection of §sec:signature_resolution (PIFB:2901–2903). A grep across PIFB lines 2945–3024 returns zero references to `sec:signature_resolution` or `sec:worked_signature`. The temporal-direction split at 2980 therefore silently imports postulates from outside the subsection's declared scope.

The §Speculative label at 3018 cannot retroactively rescue this because (i) the conjectured "1 temporal + 3 spatial" reading appears at 2980, *under* §Observable vs Subthreshold, not under §Speculative; and (ii) the §Speculative disclaimer at 3022 explicitly says "does not explain why exactly three *spatial* dimensions rather than two or four," which scopes the disclaimer to the spatial count, not to the temporal/spatial split. Blue's falsification condition (2) lists this trigger: "treats the '1 temporal + 3 spatial' decomposition as following from the spectral theorem alone (without acknowledging that the temporal-spatial split requires the signature-resolution postulates of §sec:worked_signature)." PIFB:2980 makes the split without naming the signature-resolution postulates; the fix is one citation (`see Section~\ref{sec:signature_resolution}`) inside 2945–3024, which is currently absent.

**Violation 3 — unqualified strict relativism at PIFB:3045.** The line reads: "They inhabit different phenomenal spaces while coupled through a shared noumenal substrate — all perspectives are valid." Blue's defense is that the informational coupling `β_ij = exp(-KL(q_i || Ω_{ij}[q_j]))` defuses the relativism. The coupling is real, but it does not modify the universal quantifier *all perspectives are valid*. PIFB:2937 commits to a *within-species* consensus geometry (the structural tier `G^(s)` is "the load-bearing one for an 'objective perceived geometry' reading"), which singles out one candidate among the multiplicity of `G_i`. The unqualified "all perspectives are valid" at 3045 is therefore at tension with the framework's own ontology, and matches blue's falsification condition (6): the framework invokes within-species consensus as a privileged objective geometry without the regulator-conditional caveat being preserved. The minimum honest form is "internally consistent within their own frame," not "valid" as a metaphysical absolute.

Of the three violations, Violation 1 (the `K` vs `dim(C)` conflation at 3024) is the cleanest single-sentence strike because it directly contradicts a clean statement 54 lines earlier in the same subsection, the relevant external canon [Lee2013 §11] disciplines it precisely, and blue itself listed this trigger as a falsification condition in its opening.

## Defense

My opening's primary attack was that the §Speculative-Extensions consensus/dimensional/observer-relativism triplet over-claims at three specific sentence-level locations. Blue's response was that the conditional/heuristic/speculative labels distributed through 2928, 2937, 2943, 3018, 3022 carry the right epistemic weight globally, and that 2970 disciplines the base/fiber distinction.

I grant the labels are present. The strengthening of my position is that the labels do *not* discipline the punchline. The textbook moves are:

1. **For the pullback-rank discipline:** Lee2013 *Introduction to Smooth Manifolds* §11 (Pullback of covariant tensors) establishes that `(σ^* g_B)_c` has rank at most `min(dim C, dim B)`, and the spectrum lives on `T_c C` with at most `dim(C)` eigenvalues. This is exactly what PIFB:2970 states correctly. Once stated, the framework owes consistency through to the subsection's closing claim. PIFB:3024 ("4 out of K") breaks that consistency by reverting to the fiber parameter K (= Gaussian dim, fixed at 2962) where the base parameter `n = dim(C)` is required.

2. **For the signature inheritance:** the standard real spectral theorem [HornJohnson2013 Thm 2.5.6] for a symmetric positive-semidefinite pullback `σ^* g_B` produces non-negative eigenvalues only. A Lorentzian "1 temporal + 3 spatial" split is not a property the spectral theorem delivers from a positive-semidefinite pullback; it requires an indefinite bilinear form upstream. PIFB:2901–2903 (immediately preceding §Collective Geometry) acknowledges that producing such an indefinite form in this framework requires `(i)` GL(K,C) extension, `(ii)` imaginary-frame postulate `φ_τ = iψ_τ · T`, `(iii)` real-part projection, and `(iv)` the `+tr(AB)` bilinear convention. Each of these is a postulate, not a derivation. PIFB:2980's "1 temporal + 3 spatial" silently presupposes all four. Blue's claim that the §Speculative label at 3018 and the disclaimer at 3022 do this work conflates two epistemic objects: the *count* (4) and the *signature* (1+3). The count is hypothesized; the signature is silently inherited.

3. **For the relativism:** [Friston2017 *The Graphical Brain*] does support a consensus-via-coupling reading, as blue cites. But Friston's consensus is a *narrowing* of admissible posteriors under message-passing, not a doctrine that "all perspectives are valid" without qualifier. The honest statement, matching both Friston's framework and the manuscript's own within-species consensus interpretation at 2937, is that perspectives are *internally consistent within their own frame*, not unqualifiedly valid. A publication-ready manuscript should say the latter.

My opening's red-strike conditions (a–d) from the operational reading were: (a) regulator gap glossed, (b) eigenvalue decomposition incorrectly mapped to physical dimensions, (c) 3+1 hypothesis silently inherits signature postulates, (d) gauge-as-consensus presented stronger than hypothesis. I conceded (a) and largely (d) in this rebuttal. I carry (b) via Violation 1 (the `K` vs `dim(C)` conflation at 3024 is a base/fiber conflation in the spectrum that maps a fiber count to a physical dimensional count) and (c) via Violation 2 (the "1 temporal + 3 spatial" at 2980 inherits the signature-resolution postulates without naming them).

The fix list is concrete and one-sentence-scale: at PIFB:3024 replace "4 dimensions out of K" with "4 dimensions out of n = dim(C)" (or define a separate symbol for the eigenvalue-count bound); at PIFB:2980 append a parenthetical "(see Section~\ref{sec:signature_resolution} for the postulates required to read one of these as temporal)"; at PIFB:3045 replace "all perspectives are valid" with "all perspectives are internally consistent within their own frame." Three sentence edits move the subsection from "publication-ready conditional on labels" to "publication-ready under its own stated discipline."
