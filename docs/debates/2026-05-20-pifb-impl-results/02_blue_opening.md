# Blue Opening — Defense of §Implementation and §Results Calibration

## Steelman

Red's strongest move is that the "confirmed by simultaneous increases" wording at 2308 and 2349 is a logical overstatement because the composite NE score is functionally dependent on the other three diagnostics. This is a real wording slip worth fixing, and the §Implementation "Emergent Properties" subsubsection (2169-2173) genuinely reads as untethered theoretical expectation in a section that otherwise polices its claims. Blue does not dispute these two micro-edits; the question is whether they amount to a calibration failure or to copy edits within a globally well-calibrated structure.

## Position

The single-seed scope is registered consistently and prominently throughout §Results; the threshold-detector imposition is admitted in three independent locations within §Results; the WikiText scaling numerics are reproduced verbatim from the Introduction Epistemic Status; and the phase-transition vocabulary is explicitly reserved. The §Results section is calibrated. Red's surviving points (Claim 1, Claim 2 of the red opening) are localized wording issues, not a calibration failure.

## Evidence

**Single-seed registration is dense and explicit.** Line 2301 opens with "single illustrative run (random seed 2; multi-seed reproducibility is not yet established and is flagged as a limitation in Section~\ref{sec:scope_limitations})." The Phase II subsection title at 2341 includes "(single seed)" in the title itself. Line 2344 repeats "the system in this single-seed run." Line 2353 repeats "in this single-seed run." Line 2354 repeats "in this single-seed run." Line 2428 reads "in this single-seed run" and explicitly disclaims "We do not interpret this as a critical exponent... reserve critical-exponent vocabulary for future multi-seed work." Line 2439 closes with "single-seed run does not settle." Six independent registrations across forty manuscript lines is not boilerplate; it is sustained epistemic discipline.

**Threshold-detector imposition is honestly registered.** Line 2409 (Fig. 8 caption) states "the detector that nucleates meta-agents is imposed. Whether a continuous-time evaluation of the variational criterion of Section~\ref{sec:meta_agent_variational} reproduces this structure is left for future work." Line 2430 repeats: "We do not claim free-energy-driven spontaneous emergence at this resolution, since the detector is imposed rather than derived." Line 2439 reaffirms: "the hierarchy in Fig.~\ref{fig:hierarchy} is nucleated, however, by the threshold-based consensus detector ($\Gamma_{\min} = 0.5$, $N_{\min} = 2$): without this detector no meta-agents form." The §Implementation antecedent at 2117 also volunteers that "the relation between the detector and the variational criterion is heuristic and partial rather than a derivation."

**Phase-transition vocabulary is reserved.** Line 2301 reads "consistent with a phase-transition-like reorganization, but a phase-transition claim in the statistical-mechanics sense (finite-size scaling collapse, universality, multi-seed reproducibility) is not made here; we describe the event as a reorganization in a single run and reserve the phase-transition vocabulary for future multi-seed work." This is the textbook honest delimitation: name the regime, state what would justify the stronger vocabulary, refuse it.

**WikiText scaling matches Introduction.** Line 2489-2493 gives $b = -1.049$ (CI $[-1.103, -0.998]$), $c = 61.17$, $a = 1805.55$, $R^2 \approx 0.9998$. Introduction line 125 gives "$b = -1.049$ (95\% bootstrap CI $[-1.103, -0.998]$), $R^2 \approx 0.9998$ on per-$K$ seed means with the floor parameter $c \approx 61$." Two-criterion reconciliation (bootstrap brackets at upper edge; nested $F(1,8) = 9.73$, $p = 0.014$ rejects) is reproduced in both locations. Numerics are consistent.

**Summary at 2475-2482 marks structural-versus-measured.** The Phase-III summary itemization labels each bullet "(\emph{measured})" or "(\emph{structural consequence})" and the closing paragraph at 2482 explicitly states "Items 2 and 3 are realizations of the construction rather than empirical findings; an ablation experiment... is not provided here and is left to future work." This is precisely the structural/empirical separation that distinguishes calibrated claims from inflated ones.

## Falsification conditions

Blue position falsified if: (i) any single-seed-incompatible vocabulary ("phase transition", "critical exponent", "universality") appears in §Results without disclaimer (a grep of 2248-2503 confirms it does not); (ii) the threshold-detector imposition is hidden or only mentioned once (it appears at 2409, 2430, 2439 in §Results and 2117 in §Implementation); (iii) the WikiText numerics conflict with the Introduction (they do not).

Red Claim 1 ("confirmed by simultaneous increases" wording) and Red Claim 2 (uncalibrated "Emergent Properties" subsubsection) are conceded as wording-level edits; they do not falsify the global calibration claim.
