# Red Rebuttal — pifb-observer-dependent-reality

## Concession

Blue's opening narrows the dispute correctly, and red concedes the four points blue actually establishes.

First, within-frame internal consistency holds. A single agent's pulled-back geometry $G_i$ is self-consistent, and that $G_i \neq G_j$ is a correct consequence of frame-dependence rather than an error — red's own info-geometer memo grants this.

Second, perspectival realism is a recognized, coherent middle position in philosophy of science [Massimi 2018, *Phil. & Phenom. Research* 96(2); Massimi, *Perspectival Realism*, OUP 2022]. The claim does not fail merely by occupying a band between naive objectivism and relativism.

Third, "not solipsism" is correctly the weaker claim, and blue earns it. The comparison functional $\mathrm{KL}(q_i \| \Omega_{ij}[q_j])$ is frame-invariant by Cencov uniqueness of the Fisher metric and its divergence under sufficient statistics [Cencov1972; AmariNagaoka2000 Ch. 2], so the inter-agent relation $\beta_{ij}$ encodes is a genuine frame-independent other-relation present at $N=1$. Red's variational memo (pairwise KL-closeness is weaker than shared geometry) is therefore *not* load-bearing against "not solipsism": blue's Nakahara §10.3 reading (the connection, not a base metric, compares fibers) defeats the strong eliminability framing of red's gauge-theorist and geometer memos. Red withdraws the strong "$\mathcal{C}$ is eliminable index decoration" claim. The common base is structurally needed as the shared domain of the sections; that part of blue's point 3 stands.

What red does **not** concede is that the section is "appropriately qualified." Two residues survive, and both are qualification failures, not metaphysical disputes.

## Core attack

Blue's point 5 says the conditional "is named, not buried." Granted — and beside the point. Naming a conditional does not license the *unconditional* conclusion-form that follows it. The seam is the word "therefore" at :3094. The sentence runs: the consensus construction, "*conditional on a regulator that the present manuscript does not supply*," is the framework's candidate for a shared geometry, and "the position is *therefore* one of within-frame validity and within-species consensus, not unconditional relativism." The consequent asserts present band-membership ("is therefore one of ... within-species consensus") inferred from an antecedent the same clause concedes does not exist. That inference is a non-sequitur in form: an admitted-absent candidate cannot discharge the "therefore."

This is exactly where perspectival realism's anti-relativist device fails to engage. The exit from relativism in Massimi is the perspective functioning *as a context of assessment* — claims made in one perspective actually assessed from others against world-involving standards [Massimi 2018]; and SEP fixes the general criterion: escaping relativism requires a frame-independent standard, "a neutral standpoint for adjudication between conflicting frameworks" [SEP, "Relativism"]. The manuscript's only candidate for that standard is the consensus metric, which Debate 3 established is a regulator-conditional heuristic, not a finite regulator-free gauge-invariant observable (canon chain: constant-$g$ Haar triviality [Nakahara2003 §10.1–10.4]; the local-$g(c)$ Maurer–Cartan term forcing an infinite-dimensional orbit average [PeskinSchroeder1995 §9.4]; non-compact $\mathrm{SO}(1,3)$ infinite Haar [Folland; Knapp]). A named-but-unsupplied regulator is a promissory note, not delivered cross-perspectival machinery. The honest conclusion-form is therefore "within-frame validity, plus within-species consensus *conditional on a regulator yet to be supplied*, not unconditional relativism." The text instead concludes the unconditional band-membership the conditional withdraws. That is the internal tension between the assertion and its own hedge — a wording fix the section has not made, which is precisely a failure of "appropriately qualified."

## Defense

Blue's idle-substrate rebuttal (point 3) does real work, but it defends the wrong word. Lee and Nakahara license $\mathcal{C}$ as a *smooth manifold* serving as the common base of the sections $\sigma_i, \sigma_j$, with no canonical metric descending from a fixed fiber metric absent a canonical section [Lee2013; Nakahara2003 §10.1–10.4]. Red accepts that licensing in full. But the manuscript's word is not "smooth manifold" or "base manifold" — it is "noumenal." :3094 reads "shared *noumenal* substrate"; :3098 reads "the *noumenal* manifold $\mathcal{C}$." "Noumenal" is a Kantian metaphysical category: the thing-in-itself carries mind-independent causal status ("things in themselves affect us, activating our sensible faculty," A190/A387) while remaining unknowable ("we cannot cognize things in themselves," A239) [SEP, "Kant's Transcendental Idealism"; Kant CPR]. The bundle apparatus discharges "common base index"; it does not discharge "noumenal." The gap between what the bundle role licenses (an index/domain set) and what the prose asserts (an ontological substrate with the full Kantian import) is the second place "appropriately qualified" fails — and it is unhedged, because the section itself defers the Kantian identification to a later section (:3098) while *already using* the loaded term to do realist anti-relativist work ("shared noumenal substrate"). Kant's noumenon explicitly cannot supply the metric/spatio-temporal structure that grounds a shared geometry; that structure is the subject's contribution (A42/B59–60, Bxviii). Importing the word imports a commitment the construction cannot cash.

Neither residue is a metaphysical refutation, and red does not claim the strong (non-meta) outcome — blue is right that establishing observer-dependent reality is false would require exhibiting the regulator, which is out of scope here. The meta-claim is "internally coherent *and appropriately qualified*." Coherence survives; qualification does not, on two counts. The action that follows is a REMAND with two concrete wording fixes: at :3094, move the conditional to govern the conclusion ("within-species consensus *conditional on a regulator yet to be supplied*"); at :3094/:3098, replace "noumenal substrate / noumenal manifold $\mathcal{C}$" with "base manifold $\mathcal{C}$" unless the full Kantian machinery is being imported and defended, not merely deferred.
