# Blue Opening — pifb-electron-scale-0-loadbearing

## Steelman (opposing position)

The §1.5 wording "Every system at every scale is treated as an agent ... an electron is a scale-0 agent" is a flat declarative; the §1.7 "No quantum extension" disclaimer scopes only the construction, not the ontology, so the ontological commitment to scale-0 agency is load-bearing and survives or fails on whether the framework can in fact exhibit gauge-frame structure plus variational dynamics on a quantum belief manifold; naming the density-operator manifold without exhibiting transport, inter-agent KL, or β-coupling on it is naming, not constructing, and an unconstructed ontology that pre-empts every empirical falsifier by relocating it into future research is not coherent in the sense the claim requires.

## Position

The compound claim splits as follows under the source-of-truth rule. Sub-claim (a) — that the electron example is "illustrative rather than load-bearing on the current implementation" — is correct in scope but incorrectly framed; the honest reading is that the example is **load-bearing on the ontology** (it asserts that an electron *is* an agent) and **scope-deferred on the construction** (the §1.7 disclaimer suspends the constructive instantiation, not the ontological commitment). Sub-claim (b) — that the statistical-manifold generalization beyond multivariate Gaussian is a coherent in-principle extension under canonical quantum-information-geometry — is defensible on Petz 1996 and Amari & Nagaoka 2000 ch. 7. Sub-claim (c) — that the §1.7 disclaimer bounds the constructive debt honestly — is defensible iff (a) is read in the scope-split way and the canonical precedent in the FEP literature (Friston 2019; Friston, Wiese & Hobson 2020) is taken to license pan-agentic dynamics on Markov-blanketed systems prior to constructive scale-by-scale exhibition. Net: the position is coherent under the scope-split reading of (a) plus the canonical-license reading of (b)+(c), and incoherent under any reading that demands constructive instantiation at scale-0 before the ontological commitment is admissible.

## Evidence

- **Hudson 1974 does not falsify the density-operator commitment.** Hudson, "When is the Wigner quasi-probability density non-negative?" Rep. Math. Phys. 6:249–252, shows that pure quantum states with non-negative Wigner functions are exactly the Gaussian states. This blocks the bare classical-Gaussian-on-$\mathbb{R}^K$ hosting of generic quantum states; it does not block the density-operator-manifold hosting, which accommodates any density operator $\rho \in \mathcal{D}(\mathcal{H})$ regardless of Wigner-function sign. The §1.5 edit moves to the density-operator manifold for the quantum case (`Attention/Participatory_it_from_bit.tex:119`), which is the move that defangs Hudson at the ontological level — Hudson is a constraint on representation, not on hosting.

- **Petz 1996 supplies the canonical quantum analog of the Fisher–Rao metric.** Petz, "Monotone Metrics on Matrix Spaces," Linear Algebra Appl. 244:81–96, characterizes the family of Riemannian metrics on $\mathcal{D}(\mathcal{H})$ that are monotone under stochastic (CPTP) mappings, in correspondence with operator-monotone functions $f:[0,\infty)\to\mathbb{R}$ with $f(x)=xf(x^{-1})$. The Bures and Kubo–Mori metrics are members. This is the canonical analog of the classical Fisher metric, and the data-processing inequality (Lindblad–Uhlmann monotonicity of quantum relative entropy under CPTP maps; Umegaki 1962) is the canonical analog of classical KL monotonicity under coarse-graining. The statistical-manifold side of the framework therefore has a canonically-licensed quantum extension by direct substitution.

- **Amari & Nagaoka 2000 ch. 7 develops quantum information geometry as the parallel to classical exponential families.** *Methods of Information Geometry* (AMS Translations 191) constructs the quantum analog of the dual-flat structure on $\mathcal{D}(\mathcal{H})$, including quantum exponential families and quantum mixture families with their respective dually flat affine connections. The §1.5 generalization to "any exponential family, mixture family, or in the quantum case the manifold of density operators with quantum relative entropy as the divergence" maps directly onto this canonical structure.

- **Friston 2019 and Friston, Wiese & Hobson 2020 establish the FEP-side precedent for ascribing variational dynamics universally to Markov-blanketed systems prior to scale-by-scale constructive exhibition.** Friston, "A Free Energy Principle for A Particular Physics" (arXiv:1906.10184), applies the principle at the level of particles. Friston, Wiese & Hobson, "Sentience and the Origins of Consciousness: From Cartesian Duality to Markovian Monism" (Entropy 22:516, 2020), formalizes Markovian monism as the position that any system admitting a Markov-blanket partition has internal-state dynamics interpretable as gradient flow on a variational free energy. This is a published, canonically-cited position in the FEP literature; the manuscript's pan-agentic commitment is the same structural move applied with a gauge-frame field on top.

- **Density operators host non-Gaussian states by construction.** Any quantum state (pure or mixed, Gaussian or not, with positive or negative Wigner function) has a density-operator representation $\rho \in \mathcal{D}(\mathcal{H})$ — this is definitional. Negative-Wigner states therefore live on $\mathcal{D}(\mathcal{H})$ without contradiction, and the quantum relative entropy $S(\rho \| \sigma) = \mathrm{tr}(\rho \log\rho - \rho \log\sigma)$ (Umegaki 1962) is the canonical divergence on this manifold. The §1.5 belief-manifold substitution preserves the framework's structural primitives (manifold + divergence + group action) under the unitary symmetry $\rho \mapsto U\rho U^\dagger$ available on $\mathcal{D}(\mathcal{H})$.

- **Panprotopsychism does not require committing to a specific statistical-manifold family.** Chalmers, "Panpsychism and Panprotopsychism" (2013/2015) frames panprotopsychism as the position that fundamental physical entities have proto-phenomenal structural primitives without those primitives themselves being phenomenal; the SEP entry on Panpsychism describes panprotopsychists as a family that "differ on what if anything they have to say about the positive nature of protophenomenal properties," with mysterianism cited as one option. The §1.5 commitment specifies the structural primitive (gauge-frame structure + variational dynamics on whatever statistical manifold is appropriate to scale) while leaving the family open per scale — this is panprotopsychism by exactly the structural-primitives-without-specific-phenomenal-content move that Chalmers identifies.

- **The §1.7 disclaimer scopes the constructive instantiation, the §1.5 ontology survives.** `Attention/Participatory_it_from_bit.tex:150`: "A rigorous quantum version of the framework does not currently exist; the structural parallels with QBism and other relational interpretations are suggestive rather than derived." This is a scope statement on the *construction* — what the manuscript does and does not develop. The §1.5 wording "an electron is a scale-0 agent" is a scope statement on the *ontology* — what the framework commits to as a metaphysical posture. The two are complementary scope statements; neither is collapsed to the other. The construction-debt acknowledgment at §1.7 line 150 plus the deferral to `sec:open_problems` is the manuscript's honest accounting of what remains to be exhibited.

## Falsification conditions

The position is wrong if any of the following holds.

- **Quantum relative entropy fails the data-processing inequality.** If $S(\rho\|\sigma)$ is not monotone under CPTP maps, the canonical divergence on $\mathcal{D}(\mathcal{H})$ does not behave analogously to classical KL under coarse-graining, and the substitution at the structural level fails. (This is empirically false: Lindblad 1975 and Uhlmann 1977 establish DPI; the falsifier does not bite, which is itself a strike if no other falsifier does — see closing note.)

- **No published precedent ascribes variational/gradient-flow dynamics universally across scales without constructive scale-by-scale exhibition.** If Friston 2019 / Friston-Wiese-Hobson 2020 / Sengupta et al. 2016 do not in fact license the structural move the manuscript is making, the pan-agentic commitment loses its FEP-literature precedent and reduces to a bare metaphysical claim with no canonical support. Verification anchor: the explicit quoted scope-of-FEP statements in the cited papers.

- **The density-operator manifold cannot support a coherent group-action analog of $\Omega_{ij} = \exp(\phi_i)\exp(-\phi_j)$.** If the unitary action $\rho \mapsto U\rho U^\dagger$ with $U = \exp(iH)$, $H \in \mathfrak{u}(\mathcal{H})$ does not in principle support an inter-agent transport-and-divergence kernel analogous to the classical sandwich-KL pair, the framework's gauge-frame structure has no quantum-side analog and the in-principle coherence of sub-claim (b) collapses. (The unitary action exists and is smooth; whether the resulting inter-agent quantum-KL kernel reproduces all the structural properties the classical kernel has is unconstructed in the manuscript — see concession below.)

- **Sub-claim (a) collapses to bare illustrativeness.** If "an electron is a scale-0 agent" is read as a flat illustrative example with no ontological force, then the pan-agentic commitment is vacuous and the framework reduces to its working-implementation scope (multivariate Gaussian on $\mathbb{R}^K$, classical-mesoscale agents). Red's strongest line of attack will press exactly this; my position requires the scope-split reading of (a) to be admissible, namely that the example is load-bearing ontologically and scope-deferred constructively. If the scope split is judged inadmissible — i.e., if the judge holds that an ontological commitment without constructive instantiation is not coherent in the relevant sense — the position fails.

- **Panprotopsychism without a specified statistical-manifold family is judged vacuous.** SEP §2.3 / Chalmers 2013 are tolerant of structural-primitive panprotopsychism, but if "any-statistical-manifold-appropriate-to-scale" is judged so flexible that it does no positive work, the position collapses to bare structuralism with no falsifiable ontological content. This is a philosophical-frame question rather than a canon-violation; it is the deepest of the falsifiers and the one I cannot rule out from the canon alone.

## Honest concession (volunteered, not solicited)

The manuscript does not constructively exhibit the density-operator-manifold version of the framework. Quantum relative entropy, the unitary group action on $\mathcal{D}(\mathcal{H})$, and the canonical monotone metrics of Petz 1996 are all named or available in principle, but the inter-agent transport operator, the quantum-side β-coupling, and the demonstration that the resulting quantum belief dynamics reduces appropriately to the classical-Gaussian dynamics at higher scale are not constructed. This is a research-program debt, not a contradiction; the §1.7 disclaimer is the manuscript's acknowledgment of this debt. The position the claim asks me to defend is *coherence*, not *completeness*. I defend coherence on Petz + Amari–Nagaoka ch. 7 + Friston 2019 + FWH 2020 + Chalmers 2013, and I concede the construction debt explicitly.
