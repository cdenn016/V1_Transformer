The framework commits to a stronger ontological position than the three philosophical antecedents above (Wheeler, Kant, the Free Energy Principle) jointly articulate, and that commitment is stated up front rather than buried in scope-and-limitations. Every system at every scale is treated as an agent with its own gauge frame: an electron is a scale-0 agent, a molecule a higher-scale meta-agent assembled from atomic constituents, a brain a yet-higher-scale meta-agent assembled from cellular constituents, and so on through ecosystems and societies. ## Philosophical-canon references (for positioning claims)

- Whitehead, Process and Reality (1929) — process ontology in which "actual occasions" / "actual entities" are the fundamental units; events / processes are primary rather than substances.
- Strawson 2006, "Realistic Monism: Why Physicalism Entails Panpsychism" (Journal of Consciousness Studies). Constitutive vs. emergent panpsychism. The "combination problem" (Goff 2017).
- Goff 2017, Consciousness and Fundamental Reality — combination problem for panpsychism (how do micro-experiences combine into macro-experience?).
- Chalmers 1996, The Conscious Mind; Chalmers 2016, "Panpsychism and Panprotopsychism" — the contemporary canonical articulation of panpsychism and constitutive panpsychism.
- Lahav & Neemeh 2022, "A Relativistic Theory of Consciousness" (Front. Psychol.); Lahav & Neemeh 2025 — relational physicalism, phenomenal property as observer-frame-relative.
- Russellian monism / structural realism / Russell 1927 (The Analysis of Matter).
- Rovelli 1996, "Relational Quantum Mechanics".
- Tononi 2008, 2016 — IIT and intrinsic existence.
- Friston, Wiese, Hobson 2020 / 2022 — "Sentience and the Origin of Consciousness" / Markov-blanket arguments. (Relevant because FEP applied at all scales is closely related to pan-agentic readings; Friston's "every thing has a Markov blanket" is a near-neighbor.)

## What this evidence does NOT settle

- Whether the manuscript's claim that "every system at every scale" can support a gauge frame $\phi$ is actually achievable for electrons or molecules under the framework's gauge-theoretic primitives, or whether it is an aspirational labeling without a constructive procedure (the manuscript only constructs agents at the level of "cognitive agents" / token positions in transformers; scale-0 electrons receiving a gauge frame is not exhibited).
- Whether "stronger than relational physicalism, weaker than substance idealism" is a coherent intermediate position or simply a different label for constitutive panpsychism (the manuscript explicitly disclaims panpsychism in the metaphysical sense, but the framework's commitment that scale-0 electrons "participate in the same gauge-theoretic dynamics" is closely adjacent to constitutive structural panpsychism).
- Whether the combination problem (Goff 2017) is dissolved, addressed, or simply renamed by the cross-scale aggregation mechanism (the manuscript defers it to "an open research item" — is that a coherent deferral or an evasion?).
- Whether the claim that there are "no non-agent physical things in the framework" is consistent with the manuscript's actual mathematical practice (e.g., the noumenal base manifold $\mathcal{C}$ — is the base manifold itself an agent, or a non-agent thing? If the latter, the universal-agentic claim is already false within the framework's own setup.).
