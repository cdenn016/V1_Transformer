## What this evidence does NOT settle

- Whether any prior work formally combines ALL of (i) Wheeler's participatory framing, (ii) variational free energy with explicit gauge bundles, (iii) computational instantiation as a working transformer, (iv) multi-scale renormalization-style meta-agent emergence. Each ingredient has precedent; the *conjunction* might be new. The debate must distinguish "novel conjunction" from "novel ingredients".
- Whether Sengupta et al. 2016 ("Towards a Neuronal Gauge Theory") already supplies a rigorous gauge-theoretic FEP framework that pre-empts the §1.1 line 74 priority claim. This is the most acute potential counterexample and both teams should engage with it directly.
- Whether Verlinde 2017 / entropic gravity programs already constitute "rigorous mathematical frameworks united[ing] these threads to show how 'it' can computationally emerge from 'bit'", or whether they fail the active-inference / observer-personal criterion.
- Whether the §1.4 "two radical possibilities" dichotomy (physics→cognition vs. cognition→physics) is a false binary that excludes neutral monism, dual-aspect monism, and structural realism (relevant because the introduction uses this dichotomy to motivate the cognitive-first framing).
