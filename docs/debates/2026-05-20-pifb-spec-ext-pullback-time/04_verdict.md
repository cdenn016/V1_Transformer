II §6), which is true, but the manuscript's own 2645-2646 disclosure makes clear that the horizontal block is agent-frame-dependent in a way the unqualified 2601 title and 2611 label do not register. Splitting the difference between Red and Blue is not the right call here because both teams agreed on the substantive defects; the verdict registers the agreement.
