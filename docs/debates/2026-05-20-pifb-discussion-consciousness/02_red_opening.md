# Red Opening — pifb-discussion-consciousness

## Steelman (opposing position)

The subsection is correctly calibrated because it stacks four independent hedges around every interpretive claim — a Scope paragraph at 3308-3309 inviting readers to bracket the entire move, the "highly speculative" tag at 3313, the explicit "relocation rather than solution" framing at 3337-3339, and the explanatory-limits paragraph at 3344 admitting the framework "provides mathematical vocabulary for discussing consciousness without explaining consciousness"; the qualia identification at 3324 is in-place hedged with "on the interpretive reading explored here" and "the correspondence is interpretive, not derived"; and the empirical-predictions section uses conditional verbs ("should reveal," "might show," "could test") rather than asserting the framework has predicted anything new — so the disclaimers discharge the substantive prose between them.

## Position

The subsection is mis-calibrated in two specific ways that the four-hedge structure does not discharge: (i) the framework rests entirely on Friston's Free Energy Principle / active inference machinery, yet the consciousness subsection cites no FEP-consciousness primary source — Friston 2018 "Am I self-conscious?" is the closest external comparison in the literature and is absent, while the manuscript positions itself against IIT and Russellian neutral monism, both of which are further from its actual theoretical commitments; and (ii) the qualia-indeterminacy threading at 3331 asserts the cross-scale composition $\Omega_{i,I} = \exp(\phi_i^{(s)}) \exp(-\phi_I^{(s+1)})$ "is fixed once the constituents are" without acknowledging that the constituents themselves are gauge-frame choices, so the structuralist route does not in fact locate the qualia assignment "one level down" — it relocates the gauge-fixing problem rather than solving it, and the in-place "open research question" hedge does not make this clear enough to a reader.

## Evidence

- **Manuscript text, citation gap.** `Participatory_it_from_bit.tex:3305-3354` is the consciousness subsection. A full-text search of `Participatory_it_from_bit.tex` for `Solms`, `Hidden Spring`, `Friston2018`, and `Hohwy` returns zero matches. The cited FEP / active-inference precursors in the consciousness subsection are Tononi 2004 / 2008 / 2015 / 2016, Dehaene 2014, Cleeremans 2007, Bayne 2010, Shoemaker 1982, Chalmers 1995 / 1996, Russell 1927, Strawson 2006, Goff 2017, Chalmers 2013, Mashour 2020, Luppi 2021, Gao 2017, Shine 2019, Carhart-Harris 2014, Milliere 2018, Lahav-Neemeh 2022 / 2025. None of these are FEP-grounded theories of consciousness from the Friston / active-inference lineage. The framework's foundational reference Friston 2010 is missing from this subsection's bibliography even though the subsection's substantive claim — "conscious systems correspond to meta-agents at scale $s$ that maintain explicit representations of their constituent agents at scale $s-1$ ... through free energy minimization" (3313) — is straightforwardly FEP-theoretic.

- **Primary source: Friston 2018, "Am I self-conscious? (Or does self-organization entail self-consciousness?)", *Frontiers in Psychology* 9:579.** Verified via direct fetch of the Frontiers article. Friston's central claim in that paper is "consciousness must also be a process of inference," specifically active inference about the consequences of one's own actions, and the paper explicitly grounds consciousness in hierarchical generative models that propagate "sufficient statistics between levels," with self-consciousness emerging when "generative models must entertain a distinction between self and non-self." This is the same hierarchical-sufficient-statistics machinery the manuscript invokes at 3313-3317 ("scale-2 meta-agent observing its constituent scale-1 meta-agents, which themselves observe scale-0 base processes ... maintaining beliefs about its own beliefs producing a second-order representation"). Friston 2018 is therefore the closest external comparison and the omission cannot be defended as "outside the framework's lineage."

- **Manuscript text, gauge-fixing claim at 3331.** The cross-scale composition reads $\Omega_{i,I} = \exp(\phi_i^{(s)}) \exp(-\phi_I^{(s+1)})$. The manuscript's own gauge-transformation convention at 3331 is "$\phi' = \phi + \xi$" with the induced action $G' = \Omega \cdot G \cdot \Omega^\top$ for $\Omega \in \mathrm{GL}(K)$. Under independent gauge transformations $\phi_i^{(s)} \to \phi_i^{(s)} + \xi_i^{(s)}$ and $\phi_I^{(s+1)} \to \phi_I^{(s+1)} + \xi_I^{(s+1)}$ at the two scales, the composition transforms as $\Omega_{i,I} \to \exp(\phi_i^{(s)} + \xi_i^{(s)}) \exp(-\phi_I^{(s+1)} - \xi_I^{(s+1)})$. This is not equal to $\Omega_{i,I}$ in general — the two exponentials do not commute except in abelian sub-cases — but the manuscript's free-energy invariance at 3331 is asserted for the joint transformation, so $\Omega_{i,I}$ shifts to a gauge-equivalent object rather than a unique one. The claim "the qualia assignment is then conjectured to be fixed by this concrete cross-scale constituent structure" therefore presupposes a gauge-fixing — the choice of $(\phi_i^{(s)}, \phi_I^{(s+1)})$ representatives within their gauge orbits — that the formalism at 3331 explicitly disclaims. The "open research question" hedge in the same paragraph admits this is unresolved, but the prose between leaves a reader with the impression that the structuralist route succeeds where the relational route declines, when in fact it relocates the indeterminacy one level down without resolving it.

- **Manuscript self-admission of relabeling, 3342.** The closing sentence of the Information-Geometric Structuralism paragraph reads: "The differences are real but downstream of the shared relocation move." Combined with 3342's "What is added here is a specific mathematical vocabulary in which to state that relocation ... not a new mechanism by which the relocation explains anything the predecessor positions did not already explain," this is a self-admission that the coinage is vocabulary rather than substance. The honest move on this admission is either to drop the coinage and adopt Russellian neutral monism (the closest predecessor — Russell 1927 grounds phenomenal character in intrinsic structure of a substrate non-identical with physical description, which matches the manuscript's "observer-dependent pullbacks from this substrate" almost exactly) or to identify a specific mathematical commitment the predecessors lack and lead with that. The current 3342 paragraph does neither — it lists what information-geometric structuralism does not posit (intrinsic natures, fundamental phenomenal properties, $\Phi$-identification) without stating what new explanatory work it does. The "downstream of the shared relocation move" admission undercuts the coinage's standing as a distinct position.

- **Manuscript text, qualia identification at 3324.** "The framework suggests a formal and mathematical approach to qualia ... On the interpretive reading explored here, the formal correlate of phenomenal character is the geometric structure of this induced metric. The correspondence is interpretive, not derived." The in-place hedge is genuine and reduces the strike, but the identification is still asserted declaratively as "the formal correlate" of phenomenal character — load-bearing for the rest of the subsection. The 3344 explanatory-limits paragraph admits "the relocation does not explain why the information-geometric substrate has phenomenal character" — which is the bare statement that no explanatory work has been done by the identification, only labeling. This admission, taken together with the 3342 "downstream of the shared relocation move" admission, leaves the qualia identification at 3324 doing rhetorical work that the framework's own limits paragraph disowns three paragraphs later.

The current "what is added here is a specific mathematical vocabulary" (3342) is not such a commitment because vocabulary is not new theoretical content.

If none of these falsifications carry, the minimum patch is (a) add Friston 2018, Solms 2021, and Hohwy 2022 to the consciousness-subsection bibliography with a one-paragraph statement of where the framework converges with and diverges from those positions, (b) rewrite the qualia-indeterminacy paragraph at 3331 to acknowledge that the structuralist route relocates the gauge-fixing problem rather than resolving it, and (c) either replace "information-geometric structuralism" with "Russellian neutral monism with a gauge-theoretic substrate" or state a specific mathematical commitment the coinage adds beyond vocabulary.
