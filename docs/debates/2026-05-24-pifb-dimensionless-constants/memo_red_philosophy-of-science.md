# Memo — debate-expert-philosophy-of-science (red, opening)

## Steelman of the claim
Flagging one's own interpretation as "may be unfalsifiable" (:3109) is a mark of honesty,
not evasion; and the :3133 criterion does the right thing by distinguishing failure-under-a-
complete-construction (diagnostic) from failure-under-an-incomplete-construction (non-
diagnostic), then explicitly forbidding indefinite deferral. On its face this looks Popperian.

## Falsification of the claim (the crux)
The :3133 criterion is asymmetric in disguise, an immunizing stratagem in Popper's sense and
a degenerating-programme structure in Lakatos's. The asymmetry hides in one undefined term:
"a fully developed information-geometric construction — one in which the relevant statistical
manifolds, comparison structure, and Cencov scale fixings are all specified." Nothing in the
section gives an operational criterion for when those are "all specified," nor who adjudicates
it, nor a temporal/scale-specific milestone. The result is that every actual failure to derive
α can be routed to the non-diagnostic branch ("the construction was not yet fully developed"),
because "fully developed" is judged by the same program that is on trial. That is precisely
Popper's "ad hoc auxiliary hypothesis": "Suitably imaginative adjustment of the hypotheses can
in principle render any scientific theory virtually irrefutable" (SEP "Imre Lakatos", on the
problem Popper named and Lakatos formalized).

The anti-deferral clause ("the burden is on the program … not to indefinitely defer the test
by appeal to incompleteness") has no teeth as written. Lakatos's actual demarcation requires
that "each new theory predicts novel, hitherto unexpected facts" and that "some novel
predictions are corroborated"; a programme "degenerates" when "successive theories do not
deliver novel predictions" and instead "explained away unpredicted observations after the
event" (SEP "Imre Lakatos", quoting FMSRP:34 and S&P:4–5). A progressive commitment names a
dated, scale-specific, independently checkable milestone (the way "a new particle at energy E"
is checkable). The clause at :3133 names none: no milestone, no responsible party, no time
bound, no operational test for "specified." A burden-of-proof sentence with no checkable
discharge condition is itself the unfalsifiable promise it claims to forbid.

The deeper inconsistency: :3109 concedes the parent interpretation "may be unfalsifiable …
making the framework compatible with any result by construction," then :3129 asserts "one
testable prediction emerges." An unfalsifiable parent cannot bequeath a falsifiable child
unless the child's test is fixed independently of the parent's free reinterpretation. It is
not — :3107 ("any measurement can be reinterpreted as labeling information geometry") supplies
exactly the reinterpretation latitude that lets a missed α be relabeled rather than counted
against the thesis.

## External primary-source citation
SEP, "Imre Lakatos" (plato.stanford.edu/entries/lakatos): a programme is progressive only if
"each new theory predicts novel, hitherto unexpected facts" and "some novel predictions are
corroborated"; it is degenerating when theories "do not deliver novel predictions" but instead
"explained away unpredicted 'observations' after the event" via "auxiliary hypotheses cooked up
after the event." Popper, *Logic of Scientific Discovery* §20 (immunizing stratagems / ad hoc
auxiliary hypotheses) is the parent demarcation the manuscript invokes implicitly.

## Falsification condition for THIS memo
This attack fails if the manuscript supplies, at :3133 or by cross-reference, an operational
and adjudicable definition of "fully developed construction" — a fixed-in-advance specification
of the statistical manifold, comparison structure, and Cencov scale fixing, plus a dated/bounded
milestone — such that a neutral party could declare the test "now symmetric" without the program
itself being the judge. Absent that, the criterion is degenerating-programme structure, not a
symmetric test.
