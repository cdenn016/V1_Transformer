### Philosophy of science (falsifiability / demarcation)
- SEP, "Imre Lakatos" (https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/lakatos/). A research programme is
  *progressive* only if it is "both theoretically and empirically progressive" (FMSRP:34):
  each new theory "predicts novel, hitherto unexpected facts" AND "some novel predictions are
  corroborated." It is *degenerating* when "successive theories do not deliver novel predictions
  or if the novel predictions … turn out to be false," and when it instead "explained away
  unpredicted 'observations' after the event" with "auxiliary hypotheses cooked up after the
  event." The hard core is shielded by a "protective belt of auxiliary hypotheses" that "gets
  adjusted and re-adjusted … to defend the … core" (FMSRP:48). "Suitably imaginative adjustment
  of the hypotheses can in principle render any scientific theory virtually irrefutable."
- Popper, *Logic of Scientific Discovery* (1959/2002), §20: conventionalist twists / ad hoc
  auxiliary hypotheses / immunizing stratagems; asymmetry of falsifiability vs verifiability.

### Numerical / historical record on α
- Wikipedia "Fine-structure constant" (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fine-structure_constant):
  α is a RUNNING coupling — ≈1/137.036 at low energy, "at the scale of the Z boson, about 90 GeV,
  one instead measures an effective α ≈ 1/127." Eddington (1929) "conjectured that the reciprocal
  of the fine-structure constant was … precisely the integer 137"; "by the 1940s experimental
  values for 1/α deviated sufficiently from 137 to refute Eddington's arguments." "No
  numerological explanation has ever been accepted by the physics community."
- MathWorld "Wyler's Constant" (https://mathworld.wolfram.com/WylersConstant.html): Wyler (1969)
  closed-form match to ±1.5 ppm; Adler (1972) calls it "a number in search of a theory," noting
  whether the agreement "has a basis in physics, or is purely fortuitous, remains completely
  open"; errors noted in Robertson (1971).

- SEP "Karl Popper" (https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/popper/). Advance-specification-of-

  clause is the move that makes continued deferral rationally indefensible while remaining
