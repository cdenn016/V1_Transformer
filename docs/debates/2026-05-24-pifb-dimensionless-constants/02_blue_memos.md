# Memo — debate-expert-philosophy-of-science — blue — opening — pifb-dimensionless-constants

## Lens
Falsifiability, immunizing stratagems, progressive vs degenerating research programmes,
self-disclosure of falsifiability status, manuscript-as-authority circularity.

## Frame check
The claim is that the section is HONEST, not that the framework is TRUE. These are different
epistemic objects and the distinction is the whole defense. The honesty claim is itself
falsifiable: it fails if a parallel disclaimed here as analogy is asserted elsewhere as
derivation, or if the anti-deferral clause names no concrete sub-goal. Both are checkable and
both check out (see info-geometer and the cross-reference to :2070-2088). The crux at :3133 is
correctly classified as a Lakatosian positive-heuristic commitment, not a Popperian
immunizing stratagem.

## Steelman of the opposing position
"A criterion that counts failure as evidence only 'under a fully developed construction' with
no named arbiter of 'fully developed' is a textbook immunizing stratagem: every failure is
deflected to 'not yet complete,' so the test is unfalsifiable in practice (Duhem-Quine), and
calling the parent interpretation 'may be unfalsifiable' while claiming a 'testable
prediction' from it is incoherent."

## My position (in service of blue)
The section satisfies the two demarcation virtues Popper and Lakatos prize. (1) It performs
Popper's advance specification of refutation: the parent interpretation is flagged as possibly
unfalsifiable BY THE AUTHORS, which is the good-faith disclosure Popper demands, not a defect;
and the sub-prediction's refutation conditions are laid down beforehand (:3133). (2) The
anti-deferral clause is a Lakatosian positive heuristic, not a degenerating ad hoc rescue,
because it names independently specifiable sub-goals (statistical manifolds, comparison
structure, Cencov scale fixings) whose completion is a precondition for the test rather than a
post-hoc excuse.

## Evidence
- Popper, *Logic of Scientific Discovery* / SEP "Karl Popper": "criteria of refutation have to
  be laid down beforehand: it must be agreed which observable situations, if actually observed,
  mean that the theory is refuted." The manuscript does exactly this at :3133 (success
  supports, failure under a fully-developed construction is "substantive evidence against").
- Popper, on immunization (SEP "Karl Popper"): a theory degenerates when "saved from
  falsification by the addition of ad hoc hypotheses which made it compatible with the facts."
  The manuscript's clause "the burden is on the program to develop the construction ... not to
  indefinitely defer the test by appeal to incompleteness" (:3133) is the OPPOSITE move: it
  forbids the ad hoc rescue rather than invoking one.
- Lakatos, *Methodology of Scientific Research Programmes* (FMSRP: 50): the positive heuristic
  is "a partially articulated set of suggestions or hints on how to change, develop the
  'refutable variants'." The manuscript's three named preconditions are precisely such a
  positive heuristic — a development path toward a refutable variant, not a protective belt.
- Laudan (SEP "Underdetermination"): Duhem-Quine flexibility is "psychologically possible" but
  distinct from rational "defensibility"; conflating weak underdetermination (logical
  possibility of deferral) with strong (rational equivalence) is the error. The manuscript's
  self-binding anti-deferral clause is exactly the move that makes continued deferral rationally
  indefensible even though it remains logically possible.

## Newly-discovered canon (for 01b_extended_evidence.md)
- SEP "Karl Popper" (plato.stanford.edu/entries/popper/) — advance-specification-of-refutation
  principle and the immunizing-stratagem characterization of degeneration.
- SEP "Imre Lakatos" (plato.stanford.edu/entries/lakatos/) — positive heuristic as "hints on
  how to ... develop the refutable variants" (FMSRP: 50); progressive iff theoretically and
  empirically progressive (FMSRP: 34).
- SEP "Underdetermination of Scientific Theory" (plato.stanford.edu/entries/scientific-underdetermination/)
  — Quine "come what may" (1951); Laudan's psychological-possibility-vs-rational-defensibility
  distinction.

## Falsifiability assessment of the claim
The HONESTY claim is falsified if: (a) any of the four parallels is asserted as a derivation
elsewhere in the manuscript [checked: :2070-2088 self-disclaims the mass case in the same
terms — not falsified]; (b) the anti-deferral clause names no concrete sub-goal [checked: three
named at :3133 — not falsified]; (c) the parent's "unfalsifiable" flag is leaned on as
established support elsewhere [the section instead lists it under "limitations" at :3109 — not
falsified]. The honesty claim is well-formed and falsifiable, and the available falsifiers do
not fire.

## Confidence
HIGH on the honesty claim. MEDIUM on whether the symmetric test is operationally SUFFICIENT
(no named arbiter of "fully developed"), which is an aspirational gap I concede below, not a
dishonesty.

# Memo — debate-expert-info-geometer — blue — opening — pifb-dimensionless-constants

## Lens
Fisher metric, Cencov uniqueness/scale-fixing, natural gradient, KL on statistical manifolds.

## Frame check
The mass<->Fisher identification (:3117-3119) is presented as a definitional consequence on
the statistical manifold, with the operational dispersion law explicitly NOT claimed. This is
the honest status: the Fisher matrix M and the Hessian of F coincide only on a stated slice
(isolated-agent limit), and the manuscript says so. The "Cencov scale fixings" named as a
precondition at :3133 is a real and nontrivial requirement, which is what makes the symmetric
test honest rather than vacuous.

## Steelman of the opposing position
"Identifying tr(M) with mass 'by construction' and then offering it as evidence for the
information-as-primal thesis is circular; and 'derive alpha from Fisher information scales' is
not even well-posed because Cencov's theorem fixes the Fisher metric only up to an overall
scalar, so there is no canonical scale from which a pure number like 1/137 could drop out."

## My position (in service of blue)
The manuscript's disclaimers are exactly the ones Cencov's theorem forces, which is the mark of
honesty. Cencov fixes the Fisher metric uniquely only up to a positive scalar; a dimensionless
constant therefore cannot be read off a single manifold's metric and MUST come from a RATIO of
geometric scales or a comparison structure. The manuscript names precisely this: alpha "should
emerge from RATIOS of coupling strengths or Fisher information scales" (:3131), and lists
"comparison structure" and "Cencov scale fixings" as preconditions (:3133). The section has
correctly identified that the scalar ambiguity is the obstacle and has correctly located the
test in ratios that are scalar-invariant — that is competent information geometry, honestly
stated.

## Evidence
- Cencov uniqueness theorem [Cencov1972; external_canon_math.md §1]: the Fisher metric is the
  unique Riemannian metric on a statistical manifold invariant under sufficient statistics,
  UP TO A SCALAR. The scalar freedom is exactly why a single manifold cannot yield a pure
  number and why ratios / comparison structure are required — which the manuscript names.
- KL as second-order Fisher expansion [AmariNagaoka2000 Ch. 2; external_canon_math.md §1]:
  KL ~ 1/2 (dtheta)^T g (dtheta). The mass<->Fisher coincidence at :3119 is exactly this
  second-order structure on the stated isolated-agent slice; calling it "definitional ... not
  an empirical dispersion law" is the correct characterization, not an evasion.
- Natural gradient invariance [Amari1998]: the natural gradient g^{-1} grad L is invariant
  under reparameterization; this invariance is what makes "Fisher information scales" a
  coordinate-free notion, so the proposal to extract dimensionless ratios from them is
  well-posed in principle.

## Newly-discovered canon (for 01b_extended_evidence.md)
- None beyond bibliography; Cencov1972 and Amari1998 already in external_bibliography.md.

## Falsifiability assessment of the claim
The info-geometry honesty claim fails if the manuscript claimed to extract a pure number from a
single manifold's metric (impossible under Cencov scalar freedom) or claimed an operational
M_eff vs omega^2 dispersion test it did not run. It does neither: :3131 routes through ratios,
:3119 explicitly disclaims the dispersion test. Not falsified.

## Confidence
HIGH that the disclaimers match what Cencov/Amari require. MEDIUM that the program is
COMPLETABLE — Cencov scale-fixing across DISTINCT manifolds (needed for a cross-sector ratio)
is genuinely open, which bears on the numerical-analyst's well-posedness concern.

# Memo — debate-expert-variational — blue — opening — pifb-dimensionless-constants

## Lens
VFE functional, natural-gradient descent, first-order vs second-order dynamics, ELBO/FEP.

## Frame check
The energy<->free-energy (:3121-3123) and action-principle (:3125-3127) parallels are hedged as
"analogy of form, not of derivation," and the first-order-vs-second-order distinction is the
standard one. The manuscript correctly refuses to assert F carries energy dimensions.

## Steelman of the opposing position
"Calling F a 'Hamiltonian-like' object and invoking an 'action principle' borrows physics
authority for what is just gradient descent; the rhetoric oversells a vanilla optimization
flow."

## My position (in service of blue)
The action-principle paragraph at :3125 makes exactly the distinction a careful variational
treatment must make, and makes it explicitly: the dynamics are FIRST-ORDER Riemannian
natural-gradient flow (Amari), NOT the SECOND-ORDER Euler-Lagrange/Hamilton equations of a
conservative system. The manuscript states "the resulting equations of motion have different
order and a different ontological reading: first-order descent on a statistical manifold versus
second-order conservative dynamics on a configuration manifold" (:3125). That is the correct
and load-bearing disanalogy, volunteered rather than hidden. A dishonest section would have
elided the order mismatch to make the physics analogy look tighter; this one foregrounds it.

## Evidence
- Natural gradient is first-order steepest descent in the Fisher-Riemannian sense [Amari1998;
  external_canon_math.md §1]. Hamilton's principle yields second-order Euler-Lagrange
  equations. The orders genuinely differ; the manuscript's :3125 statement is correct.
- Free energy in the FEP sense F = E_q[log q - log p(o,s)] = KL(q||p(s|o)) - log p(o)
  [Friston2010] is an information-theoretic functional measured in nats, NOT a quantity with
  joules dimension. The manuscript's refusal at :3123 ("we have not established that F has
  energy dimensions") is consistent with the canonical FEP definition.
- The variational principle delta F = 0 defines critical points of a scalar functional; that
  this FORM is shared with Hamilton's principle is true but content-free without a kinetic term
  — which the manuscript separately flags as a postulate at :2088 ("This is a postulate, not a
  consequence of F").

## Newly-discovered canon (for 01b_extended_evidence.md)
- None beyond bibliography; Amari1998 and Friston2010 already present.

## Falsifiability assessment of the claim
Fails if the manuscript claimed second-order conservative dynamics, or claimed F has energy
units, or claimed a derived Lagrangian. It claims none — :3125 and :3127 explicitly disclaim
each. Not falsified.

## Confidence
HIGH. The order distinction is textbook and the manuscript states it verbatim.

smuggle in the physical content (curvature dynamics, gauge-invariant observables) that real
gauge theory would require.

## Evidence

# Memo — debate-expert-numerical-analyst — blue — opening — pifb-dimensionless-constants

## Lens
Well-posedness / computability of the proposed derivation; whether a failure could be diagnostic.

## Frame check
The crux's symmetry depends on the failure mode being COMPUTABLE: failure "under a fully
developed construction" can only be diagnostic if such a construction is, in principle, a
finite specified computation. The manuscript names the ingredients (statistical manifolds,
comparison structure, Cencov scale fixings) but supplies no decision procedure or arbiter for
"fully developed."

## Steelman of the opposing position
"There is no operational stopping rule: no test that returns 'construction complete, failure is
now diagnostic.' Without it, 'fully developed' is unbounded and the symmetric test never
triggers — the criterion is aspirational, not operational."

## My position (in service of blue, with explicit concession)
On the narrow numerical question I find limited independent traction: this is a
philosophy-of-honesty claim, and the well-posedness of an unwritten future derivation cannot be
adjudicated numerically. What I CAN certify is that the named preconditions are concrete enough
to be checkable in principle — a specified statistical manifold, a specified comparison map, and
a specified Cencov scalar fixing are each finite objects whose presence or absence is
decidable. That converts "fully developed" from a pure rhetorical hedge into a checklist. I
therefore SUPPORT the honesty claim weakly and CONCEDE the gap: the manuscript names no party
who certifies the checklist complete, so the symmetric test's trigger is aspirational until the
three preconditions are concretely instantiated. This memo is otherwise the weakest of the
five and should be discounted relative to philosophy and info-geometry in synthesis.

## Evidence
- Cencov scale-fixing [Cencov1972; external_canon_math.md §1] is a concrete, finite operation
  (choosing the scalar on each manifold); its specification is decidable, so naming it as a
  precondition is not vacuous.
- Reparameterization invariance of natural gradient [Amari1998] means the proposed ratios, IF
  the manifolds and comparison map are specified, are coordinate-free and hence computable
  without an arbitrary chart choice — the computation is well-posed once inputs are fixed.

## Newly-discovered canon (for 01b_extended_evidence.md)
- None beyond bibliography.

## Falsifiability assessment of the claim
The honesty claim survives; the SUFFICIENCY of the symmetric test does not, absent a stopping
rule. I concede this as the live limit of the defense.

## Confidence
LOW as an independent lens (philosophy-of-honesty claim); the substantive content folds into
the philosophy and info-geometry memos. Explicitly discounted in synthesis weight.
