## What this evidence does NOT settle

- Whether "fully developed information-geometric construction" is DEFINED tightly enough that "not yet fully developed" cannot be invoked indefinitely — i.e., whether the anti-deferral clause has teeth or is itself unfalsifiable (who decides when the construction is "fully developed"?).
- Whether flagging the parent interpretation as "may be unfalsifiable" (:3109) while claiming "one testable prediction emerges" (:3129) is consistent — can an unfalsifiable interpretation have a falsifiable sub-prediction, and does deriving α actually test the information-as-primal thesis or just exhibit a numerical coincidence?
- Whether the structural parallels' hedging is COMPLETE or whether any parallel slips from analogy into asserted derivation.
- Whether the mass↔Fisher "definitional, no operational test" disclosure (:3119) is fully consistent with how the mass analogy is used elsewhere (the project's mass-TODO suggests the operational test is open; the manuscript here correctly disclaims it).
