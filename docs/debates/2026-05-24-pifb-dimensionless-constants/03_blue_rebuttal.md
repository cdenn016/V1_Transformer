# Blue Rebuttal — pifb-dimensionless-constants

## Concession

We grant the precise residue of red's Vector 1. The claim under evaluation says the
dimensionless-constants criterion is "GENUINELY SYMMETRIC" (:3133). Red is right that the
section names no arbiter — no party, no dated milestone, no stopping rule that returns
"construction now complete, a further α-miss is diagnostic." Without such an arbiter the trigger
for the diagnostic branch is not operationally fixed. The honest word is therefore not
"genuinely symmetric" but "aspirationally / conditionally symmetric": the criterion becomes a
genuine symmetric test only once the three named preconditions (statistical manifolds,
comparison structure, Cencov scale fixings) AND a completion criterion or neutral arbiter for
"fully developed" are concretely specified. This is a one-word remand — "genuinely" →
"aspirationally, pending the specification of [the three preconditions] and an arbiter of
completion" — and we grant it without reservation.

We bound the concession exactly there. It does not extend to "the criterion is empty," "the test
is immunizing," or "failure can be deflected forever." The numerical-analyst's opening already
isolated this gap: the named preconditions are each finite, decidable objects — a specified
manifold, a specified comparison map, a specified scalar fixing are each present-or-absent by
inspection — so "fully developed" is a checklist with a missing certifier, not an unbounded
rhetorical hedge. The missing certifier is a real gap. It is the only one.

## Core attack

Red's load-bearing error is a scope substitution running through Vectors 2 and 3: red attacks
whether α is *derivable* and whether any α-derivation could *confirm a metaphysics*, when the
claim on trial (00_claim.md) is whether the criterion is *honestly characterized*. These are
different epistemic objects, and the manuscript itself separates them.

Vector 3 (Eddington/Wyler) is the clearest substitution. The historical record that "no
numerological explanation has ever been accepted by the physics community" and that even Wyler's
ppm match was dismissed as "a number in search of a theory" (Adler 1972; Wikipedia
"Fine-structure constant," in 01b_extended_evidence.md) is an argument about the *confirmation
power* of any α-derivation — a claim about the success branch's evidential force in general. It
does not bear on whether the section's prose honestly states its own status. The section already
concedes the parent interpretation "may be unfalsifiable" (:3109) and that "we have not derived
numerical values of physical constants" (:3109). A historical argument that α-derivations don't
confirm metaphysics agrees with the manuscript's own posted caution; it does not catch the
section overstating.

Vector 2 needs a sharper answer because it does touch the success branch, but it confirms the
concession rather than refuting the claim. Red enumerates free choices — the Cencov scalar, the
comparison-structure connection, the VFE couplings — and concludes a match would be curve-fitting.
Grant every item: those choices *are* open right now. But the symmetric test at :3133 explicitly
*requires those very choices to be specified in advance* — "one in which the relevant statistical
manifolds, comparison structure, and Cencov scale fixings are all specified." Red's Vector 2 is
the statement that the preconditions are not yet met. That is precisely what the manuscript's
"fully developed" clause names as the threshold the program must reach before any α-result counts
either way. Vector 2 therefore shows the symmetric test has not yet *triggered*; it does not show
the criterion *would not be symmetric once the preconditions are fixed*. "Choices open today,
required to be fixed before the test" is not "free parameters tunable after seeing the answer" —
the criterion's own precondition clause forbids the post-hoc move red attributes to it.

Information geometry sharpens this into a point in the manuscript's favor. Čencov's theorem fixes
the Fisher–Rao metric uniquely only up to a positive multiplicative scalar (Chentsov's theorem,
Čencov 1972/1981; Ay–Jost–Lê–Schwachhöfer, arXiv:1207.6736, "unique up to a constant," in
01b_extended_evidence.md). A pure number therefore *cannot* be read off a single manifold's
metric — it must come from a scalar-invariant *ratio* or a comparison structure. The manuscript
routes exactly there: α "should emerge from ratios of coupling strengths or Fisher information
scales" (:3131). Red treats "ratios … or Fisher information scales" as an open schema that admits
post-hoc tuning; under Čencov it is the *forced and correct* response to the scalar ambiguity.
The section has located the obstacle (scalar freedom) and the only admissible escape (ratios)
correctly. That is competent information geometry honestly stated, which is what the claim
asserts.

## Defense

The honesty claim survives because the section exhibits the two demarcation virtues Popper and
Lakatos prize, and the concession above is the full extent of what red establishes.

First, advance specification of refutation. Popper requires that "criteria of refutation have to
be laid down beforehand: it must be agreed which observable situations, if actually observed,
mean that the theory is refuted" (SEP "Karl Popper," in 01b_extended_evidence.md). The section
does this twice over: it flags the parent interpretation as possibly unfalsifiable *by the
authors themselves* under "limitations" (:3109) — the candid self-disclosure Popper demands, not
a defect — and it lays down the sub-prediction's refutation conditions before any result is in
(:3133). Self-flagging one's own parent thesis as possibly unfalsifiable is a Popperian virtue,
not the incoherence red alleges; an unfalsifiable parent can carry a falsifiable child precisely
when the child's refutation conditions are fixed independently and in advance, which :3133 does.

Second, and decisive against the immunizing-stratagem charge, the structural distinction red's
Vector 1 elides. A *positive heuristic* in Lakatos is "a partially articulated set of suggestions
or hints on how to change, develop the 'refutable variants'" (FMSRP:50, SEP "Imre Lakatos," in
01b_extended_evidence.md) — a development path specified *toward* a refutable test. An *immunizing
stratagem* is the opposite temporal move: an ad hoc auxiliary invented *after* a refutation to
shield the core ("saved from falsification by the addition of ad hoc hypotheses which made it
compatible with the facts," SEP "Karl Popper"). The manuscript's clause is structurally the
former. Its three preconditions are named in advance as a development path, and its anti-deferral
sentence — "the burden is on the program to develop the construction to the point where the test
is symmetric, not to indefinitely defer the test by appeal to incompleteness" (:3133) — *forbids*
the after-the-fact rescue rather than invoking one. A section that explicitly prohibits the
immunizing move is not executing it. Laudan's distinction closes the loop: continued deferral is
"psychologically possible" but the self-binding anti-deferral clause is exactly what makes it
rationally indefensible (SEP "Underdetermination," in 01b_extended_evidence.md). Red's Vector 1
conflates the logical possibility of deferral (always available, Duhem–Quine) with its rational
defensibility (which the clause removes).

Third, the honesty is non-local, which is the cross-check that distinguishes an honest section
from a locally polished one. The mass↔Fisher disclaimer at :3119 ("a definitional consequence …
not an empirical dispersion law … no operationally independent measurement is reported") is not a
one-off hedge: the mass-analogy's *source* section self-disclaims in the same terms —
"interpretive within the framework rather than a derivation of physical inertial mass" and "This
is a postulate, not a consequence of F" (:2070–2088, consistency check in 01b_extended_evidence.md,
not cited as canonical authority). The disclaimer at the section under review matches how the
analogy is characterized at its origin. And the order-distinction in the action parallel —
first-order Riemannian natural-gradient flow (Amari 1998, M⁻¹∇F, a reparameterization-invariant
rescaling of the descent direction with no intrinsic conserved scalar) versus second-order
Hamilton dynamics (:3125) — is the load-bearing disanalogy a dishonest section would have hidden
to tighten the physics parallel; this one foregrounds it. Free couplings in a first-order flow
manufacture no parameter-free scalar, which is exactly why the manuscript says it "has not
derived numerical values" (:3109) — red's free-parameter finding *confirms* the disclaimer rather
than catching an overstatement.

Falsification conditions for this blue position, stated explicitly. The honesty claim is *not*
defensible if any of the following holds: (a) any of the four parallels is asserted as a
*derivation* somewhere else in the manuscript — checked at :2070–2088, which self-disclaims in
the same terms, so this does not fire; (b) the anti-deferral clause names *no* concrete sub-goal,
making it a bare unfalsifiable promise — checked, three preconditions are named at :3133, so this
does not fire; (c) the parent's "unfalsifiable" flag is leaned on *as established support*
elsewhere — checked, the section files it under limitations at :3109, so this does not fire. All
three available falsifiers are checkable and none fires. The one residue that does survive is the
missing arbiter conceded above — which downgrades the word "genuinely" to "aspirationally," a
remand, not a refutation.

We accept one contested residue openly. The scope-limit that quarantines Vector 3 — that the
claim concerns the criterion's *honest characterization*, not the program's *ultimate
confirmation power* — is itself a contestable framing; a judge who reads the claim as warranting
the program's eventual success would weigh Eddington/Wyler against it. We do not paper over this.
But the claim's text (00_claim.md) is about honesty and symmetry of the *criterion*, and on that
object the section delivers exactly the advance-specified, anti-immunizing, non-locally consistent
disclosure the honesty claim asserts — modulo the one-word remand on "genuinely."
