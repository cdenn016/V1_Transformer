# Red Opening — pifb-dimensionless-constants

## Steelman (opposing position)

The §"Physical Quantities and Dimensionless Constants" treatment is unusually honest for a
speculative section: it flags its own parent interpretation as "may be unfalsifiable" (:3109),
hedges every structural parallel as a "formal analogy without quantitative predictions" (:3113),
discloses the mass↔Fisher identification as definitional with "no operationally independent
measurement" (:3119), correctly separates first-order natural-gradient flow from second-order
Hamilton dynamics (:3125), and then proposes a dimensionless-constants program whose
success-failure criterion is explicitly built to be symmetric and explicitly forbids indefinite
deferral (:3133).

## Position

The §"Dimensionless Constants" claim of a "genuinely symmetric success-failure criterion"
(:3133) is false. The criterion is asymmetric in both directions and is, in Lakatos's terms,
the structure of a degenerating research programme rather than a Popperian test. Failure is
non-falsifying because "fully developed construction" is undefined and adjudicated by the program
itself; success is non-confirming because the route to α has free real parameters (Cencov scale
constant, comparison-structure / connection choice, VFE coupling weights, and even the
renormalization scale of the target) that can be set after seeing the answer, and because the
historical record shows even ppm-accurate pure-structure derivations of α do not confirm any
metaphysics. A test whose failure cannot refute and whose success cannot confirm is empty, not
symmetric. The honest disclaimers elsewhere in the section (which we grant) do not rescue this;
they relocate the entire predictive burden onto the one prediction the section fails to
discipline.

## Evidence

### Vector 1 — Failure side: "fully developed" is an undefined, self-adjudicated escape hatch (lead, philosophy-of-science)

The criterion at :3133 partitions outcomes by one undefined term: failure "under a fully
developed information-geometric construction — one in which the relevant statistical manifolds,
comparison structure, and Cencov scale fixings are all specified" is diagnostic, while "failure
under an incomplete construction is non-diagnostic." Nothing in the section gives an operational
test for when those preconditions are "all specified," names a responsible adjudicator, or sets
a dated, scale-specific milestone. Every actual α-miss can therefore be routed to the
non-diagnostic branch — "the construction was not yet fully developed" — and that routing is
judged by the same program on trial. This is exactly Popper's conventionalist twist: "Suitably
imaginative adjustment of the hypotheses can in principle render any scientific theory virtually
irrefutable" (SEP "Imre Lakatos," on the immunizing stratagem Popper named in *Logic of
Scientific Discovery* §20). Lakatos's demarcation requires that a programme "predicts novel,
hitherto unexpected facts" with "some novel predictions … corroborated," and brands as
degenerating any programme that instead "explained away unpredicted 'observations' after the
event" with "auxiliary hypotheses cooked up after the event" (SEP "Imre Lakatos," quoting
FMSRP:34, S&P:4–5). A genuine progressive commitment names a checkable, bounded milestone; the
anti-deferral clause at :3133 ("the burden is on the program … not to indefinitely defer")
names none — no milestone, no adjudicator, no time bound, no operational definition of
"specified." A burden-of-proof sentence with no checkable discharge condition is itself the
unfalsifiable promise it purports to forbid.

This compounds an internal inconsistency: :3109 concedes the parent interpretation "may be
unfalsifiable as any measurement can be reinterpreted as labeling information geometry, making
the framework compatible with any result by construction," yet :3129 asserts "one testable
prediction emerges." An unfalsifiable parent cannot bequeath a falsifiable child unless the
child's test is fixed independently of the parent's reinterpretive latitude. It is not — the
"nats are fundamental, kg is a map-label" stance (:3107) supplies precisely the latitude that
lets a missed α be relabeled rather than counted against the thesis.

### Vector 2 — Success side: the route to α has free real parameters, so a match is curve-fitting, not a test (info-geometer + gauge-theorist + variational)

The :3133 preconditions are not constraints the geometry hands you; each is a free choice the
program must supply, and the freedom is exactly what makes a post-hoc match reachable.

"Cencov scale fixings" names a free constant. Čencov's theorem fixes the Fisher–Rao metric as
the unique metric invariant under sufficient statistics ONLY UP TO A POSITIVE MULTIPLICATIVE
CONSTANT (Chentsov's theorem, Čencov 1972/1981; Ay–Jost–Lê–Schwachhöfer, arXiv:1207.6736:
unique "up to a constant"). The proposed route is "ratios of coupling strengths or Fisher
information scales" (:3131). A ratio cancels the Cencov constant only when the same constant is
used on the same model; the moment the named "comparison structure" (:3133) compares two
different statistical models — not two coordinate patches on one model, but distinct manifolds
or irrep sectors with independent Fisher metrics — their relative scaling is not fixed by
Čencov's theorem, and the ratio is tunable.

"Comparison structure" names a free connection. In this framework agent-to-agent comparison is
the transport Ω_ij = exp(φ_i)exp(−φ_j), and a connection on a principal bundle is independent
additional structure, not fixed by the base manifold — parallel transport and holonomy are
connection-dependent ([Nakahara2003] Ch. 10–11). The framework already admits multiple such
choices (flat vs non-flat transport, distinct irrep dimensions, GL(K,R) vs the GL(K,C) signature
pathway), and :3109 explicitly concedes that the temporal generator in that pathway is "imposed
by hand." Gauge freedom fixed by hand is the textbook mechanism for engineering a post-hoc
match: choose the gauge that makes the number come out, then declare the construction "fully
developed."

The VFE substrate itself is parameter-laden. The framework's canonical free energy carries free
coupling weights (α, λ_h, β_ij, γ_ij, τ), and the dynamics are, by the manuscript's own
statement, "first-order Riemannian natural-gradient flow" (:3125) with no derived Lagrangian and
no conserved Hamiltonian. The natural gradient M^{-1}∇F only rescales the descent direction
([Amari1998]); it manufactures no absolute, scale-fixed pure number. A first-order flow with
freely chosen couplings has no canonical parameter-free scalar from which a unique α could fall
out. The same freedom that powers the honestly-hedged analogies of :3117–:3125 absorbs any
α-miss.

### Vector 3 — Success side, empirical record: even a ppm match does not confirm (numerical-analyst)

The target is not a fixed number. α is a running coupling: ≈1/137.036 at low energy, but "at the
scale of the Z boson, about 90 GeV, one instead measures an effective α ≈ 1/127" (Wikipedia
"Fine-structure constant," standard QED running). "Deriving 1/137" silently chooses the
low-energy scale post hoc — an extra free input the :3131 schema does not fix. And the historical
record is decisive against the claimed symmetry on the success side: Eddington (1929)
"conjectured that the reciprocal of the fine-structure constant was … precisely the integer 137"
and tried to derive it from first principles; "by the 1940s experimental values for 1/α deviated
sufficiently from 137 to refute Eddington's arguments." Wyler (1969) produced a closed-form match
to ±1.5 ppm that Adler (1972) dismissed as "a number in search of a theory," noting the agreement
might be "purely fortuitous"; "no numerological explanation has ever been accepted by the physics
community" (Wikipedia "Fine-structure constant"; MathWorld "Wyler's Constant"). So a successful
α-derivation, even to ppm, has repeatedly failed to confirm any particular metaphysics. The
manuscript's symmetry breaks at both ends: failure → "not fully developed" (Vector 1), success →
one of indefinitely many geometric coincidences (Vector 3).

## Concession

We grant, without reservation, the points the claim correctly identifies as honest. Flagging the
parent interpretation as "may be unfalsifiable" (:3109) is candid self-disclosure, not evasion.
The four structural parallels are consistently hedged as "formal analogies without quantitative
predictions" (:3113). The mass↔Fisher disclosure at :3119 — "a definitional consequence of that
identification rather than an empirical dispersion law, and no operationally independent
measurement is reported" — is honest and matches the project's open mass-TODO. The action-
principle parallel at :3125 correctly distinguishes first-order Riemannian natural-gradient flow
([Amari1998]) from the second-order Euler–Lagrange/Hamilton dynamics of a configuration manifold;
that is "analogy of form, not of derivation," stated correctly. None of these concessions touches
the crux. They concern the parallels and the parent interpretation; the crux is the one place the
section drops its discipline — the :3133 claim that the α-program's criterion is symmetric.

## Falsification conditions

This red position is wrong if any of the following holds.

1. The manuscript supplies (at :3133 or by cross-reference) an operational, adjudicable
   definition of "fully developed construction" — a specification of the statistical manifold,
   comparison structure, and Cencov scale fixing fixed in advance, plus a bounded, neutrally
   checkable milestone — such that a party other than the program could declare the test "now
   symmetric." Absent this, Vector 1 stands.

2. The manuscript exhibits a route to α in which the Cencov constant cancels identically, the
   comparison structure / connection is fixed by a stated invariance principle (not chosen), the
   gauge group and irrep are fixed in advance, and the VFE coupling weights do not enter — i.e.,
   a derivation with zero free real parameters after the canonical Fisher metric is imposed.
   "Ratios of coupling strengths or Fisher information scales" (:3131) is a schema with open
   choices, so Vector 2 stands.

3. The program pre-registers the renormalization scale and a target precision with error bars,
   then a parameter-free derivation hits that target within stated error as a point prediction.
   The :3131 text fixes neither scale nor precision, and the Eddington/Wyler record shows even
   ppm matches have not confirmed, so Vector 3 stands.

If none of these is met, the §"Dimensionless Constants" criterion is a degenerating-programme
immunizing stratagem dressed as a symmetric test, and the claim of genuine symmetry fails.
