# Blue Opening — pifb-dimensionless-constants

## Steelman (opposing position)

The strongest form of the attack: the dimensionless-constants criterion is an immunizing
stratagem dressed as a Popperian test, because "failure under a fully developed construction is
substantive evidence against, failure under an incomplete construction is non-diagnostic"
(:3133) supplies no arbiter of "fully developed," so by Duhem-Quine any failure is deflectable
to "the construction was not yet complete" indefinitely; and flagging the parent interpretation
as "may be unfalsifiable" (:3109) while claiming "one testable prediction emerges" (:3129) is
incoherent, since an unfalsifiable parent cannot license a falsifiable child.

## Position

The treatment at :3100-3134 is HONEST in the precise sense the claim asserts, and the honesty
claim is itself falsifiable and survives its falsifiers. Three things are simultaneously true
and are exactly what an honest speculative section should display: the parent interpretation is
disclosed BY THE AUTHORS as possibly unfalsifiable and is filed under limitations rather than
leaned on as support; the four structural parallels are hedged as form-not-derivation, and the
one parallel with an origin section elsewhere (mass) is disclaimed there in identical terms;
and the dimensionless-constants criterion is a Lakatosian positive heuristic — a self-binding
commitment that names a development path and forbids indefinite deferral — not a protective
belt invented after a refutation. The defense concedes one real gap, stated below: the criterion
names no party who certifies "fully developed," so its symmetric trigger is aspirational until
the three preconditions are concretely instantiated. That is an incompleteness, not a
dishonesty, and the manuscript flags it as such.

## Evidence

### Vector 1 — The crux at :3133 is a Lakatosian positive heuristic, not an immunizing stratagem

Lakatos's positive heuristic is "a partially articulated set of suggestions or hints on how to
change, develop the 'refutable variants'" (FMSRP: 50; SEP "Imre Lakatos"). The manuscript's
clause names three concrete development targets — "the relevant statistical manifolds,
comparison structure, and Cencov scale fixings are all specified" (:3133) — and states that
"the burden is on the program to develop the construction to the point where the test is
symmetric, not to indefinitely defer the test by appeal to incompleteness" (:3133). That is the
positive-heuristic category exactly: a path toward a refutable variant. The immunizing
stratagem Popper warns against is the opposite operation — a theory "saved from falsification by
the addition of ad hoc hypotheses which made it compatible with the facts" (SEP "Karl Popper").
The manuscript ADDS NO hypothesis to absorb a failure; it instead pre-commits to a derivation
and disowns the very escape (indefinite deferral) the attack accuses it of reserving. The
distinction is structural: an immunizing stratagem is invoked AFTER an anomaly to protect a
hard core; a positive heuristic is stated BEFORE, to direct development. This clause is stated
before any derivation is attempted.

The Duhem-Quine half of the attack is real but answered by its own literature. Quine's "come
what may" preservation (SEP "Underdetermination") is, in Laudan's correction, "psychologically
possible" but distinct from rational "defensibility"; the error is "conflating weak
underdetermination (logical possibility) with strong (rational equivalence)." The manuscript's
anti-deferral clause is precisely the device that makes continued deferral logically possible
but rationally indefensible — it pre-commits the program to stop deferring. An author who
intended the escape would not write the sentence that forecloses it.

### Vector 2 — Self-disclosure of unfalsifiability is the Popperian virtue, and the parent/child split is coherent

Popper demands that "criteria of refutation have to be laid down beforehand" (SEP "Karl
Popper"). The manuscript does two creditable things at once. It lays down the sub-prediction's
refutation criterion in advance (:3133), and it volunteers that the PARENT interpretation "may
be unfalsifiable as any measurement can be reinterpreted as labeling information geometry"
(:3109) — filing this under "this stance faces limitations," not under support. Self-reporting
that one's own metaphysical postulate may be unfalsifiable is the good-faith disclosure Popper's
demarcation rewards, not a defect to be penalized.

The parent/child coherence the attack denies is standard. An unfalsifiable framing interpretation
can carry a falsifiable sub-commitment: the interpretation is unfalsifiable as a global
relabeling, yet it COMMITS to a specific consequence (dimensionless constants derivable from
information geometry) whose failure under a specified construction would count against it. The
manuscript states exactly this conditional structure — success "would substantively support ...
by exhibiting an instance of the derivation the interpretation commits to," failure under a
fully developed construction "would constitute substantive evidence against ... since the
interpretation is committed to such a derivation being possible" (:3133). The information-
geometry lens certifies the sub-commitment is well-posed in principle: natural gradient is
reparameterization-invariant [Amari1998], so once the manifolds and comparison map are fixed,
the proposed ratios are coordinate-free and computable; and Cencov's uniqueness theorem fixes
the Fisher metric only up to a positive scalar [Cencov1972; external_canon_math.md §1], which is
exactly why a dimensionless constant must come from a RATIO rather than a single manifold's
metric — and exactly what the manuscript routes through ("ratios of coupling strengths or Fisher
information scales," :3131). The section has identified the real obstacle (scalar freedom) and
located the test where it survives that obstacle.

### Vector 3 — The honesty is not local: the mass disclaimer holds at its origin

The most checkable falsifier of the honesty claim is whether a parallel disclaimed here as
analogy is asserted as derivation elsewhere. The mass↔Fisher case is the live one, given the
project's open mass-TODO. It checks out. At :3119 the section disclaims "no operationally
independent measurement is reported" and calls the M_eff ∝ Σ_p^{-1} scaling "a definitional
consequence of that identification rather than an empirical dispersion law." The ORIGIN section,
:2070-2088, disclaims in identical terms: the mass identification is "interpretive within the
framework rather than a derivation of physical inertial mass" (:2072); the harmonic scaling is
"a definitional consequence of the postulate rather than an independent dynamical scaling"
(:2088); and the velocity-quadratic kinetic form is headed "This is a postulate, not a
consequence of F" (:2088). The variational lens confirms the energy and action parallels are
hedged with the same restraint: the manuscript states the dynamics are "first-order ...
natural-gradient flow of Amari" and NOT "second-order Euler-Lagrange equations of Hamilton's
principle," labelling the relation "analogy of form ... rather than of derivation" (:3125),
which is the correct order distinction [Amari1998] volunteered rather than hidden; and it
refuses to claim F carries energy dimensions (:3123), consistent with the canonical FEP
definition of free energy as an information functional in nats [Friston2010]. The gauge lens
adds that the "Lie-group natural gradient ... via the right-trivialized group retraction"
named at :3125 is a standard manifold-optimization object [AbsilMahonySepulchre2008], used to
describe the optimizer's geometry, with no curvature-coupled physical field equation or
gauge-invariant physical observable smuggled in [Nakahara2003 §10-11]. Every parallel is hedged,
and the hedging is consistent across the sections that share each object.

### Expert disposition

Philosophy-of-science (lead), info-geometer, variational, and gauge-theorist each supply a
load-bearing citation above. The numerical-analyst memo is explicitly DISCOUNTED: on a
philosophy-of-honesty claim it found limited independent traction, and its one substantive
contribution (that the three named preconditions are finite, checkable objects, so "fully
developed" is a checklist rather than pure rhetoric) folds into Vector 1 and the concession
below.

## Concession (the live limit of this defense)

The manuscript names no arbiter for "fully developed." Three preconditions are named (:3133),
and each is a finite, decidable object — but no party is designated to certify the checklist
complete, and Cencov scale-fixing ACROSS distinct manifolds (required for a cross-sector ratio
like α) is itself open. The symmetric test's trigger is therefore aspirational: it would become
operational only once the three preconditions are concretely instantiated and someone is
empowered to declare them so. This is the honest discriminator from a true immunizing
stratagem: the manuscript names the preconditions and disowns deferral (more than most
speculative programs offer), but it does not specify WHO decides when deferral has become
illegitimate. The honesty claim survives; the SUFFICIENCY of the symmetric criterion does not,
and the manuscript should not be read as having delivered an operational test, only a
self-binding commitment to build one.

## Falsification conditions

This defense is wrong if any of the following holds:

1. Any of the four structural parallels (time, mass, energy, action) is asserted ELSEWHERE in
   the manuscript as a derivation rather than an analogy. Checked for the mass case
   (:2070-2088) — disclaimed in identical terms; the falsifier does not fire there. If the
   red team exhibits such an assertion for time, energy, or action elsewhere, the honesty claim
   collapses on that parallel.

2. The anti-deferral clause is operationally vacuous — i.e., names no concrete sub-goal whose
   non-completion would count as deferral. Checked: three sub-goals named at :3133. The
   falsifier does not fire on existence; it fires only if "fully developed" is shown to be
   unbounded in a way the three preconditions do not constrain — which is the concession above,
   and bears on SUFFICIENCY, not on honesty.

3. The mass↔Fisher identification (:3119) is invoked elsewhere with empirical-test or
   dispersion-law STATUS despite being disclaimed as definitional here. Checked against
   :2070-2088 — invoked there as a postulate/definitional reading, with the operational ω test
   explicitly "deferred to future work" (:2088). The falsifier does not fire.

4. The parent's "may be unfalsifiable" flag (:3109) is leaned on as ESTABLISHED support for the
   framework anywhere in the section. Checked: it appears under "this stance faces limitations"
   (:3109); the section draws no positive support from it. The falsifier does not fire.

If the red team produces an in-manuscript instance of (1), (3), or (4), the honesty claim is
defeated on that point. Absent such an instance, the residual disagreement is confined to (2)'s
sufficiency horn — which is a conceded incompleteness, not a dishonesty, and on the honesty
question as posed the defense holds.
