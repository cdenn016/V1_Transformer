# Verdict — pifb-scale-dependent-time

## Outcome

REMAND

## Decisive evidence

Three separable defects, each pinned to a manuscript line and each conceded by Blue or established by Red against the canon, against a remainder of components that survive intact:

1. **Line 2649 prose (the one live dispute).** The text reads "incoherent injection, with power spread uniformly over all $N$ eigenmodes, projects onto this single mode with weight $1/N$, **which is the spectral origin of $Z_I = 1/N$**" (`Attention/Participatory_it_from_bit.tex:2649`). Red's rebuttal Case B falsifies the stated mechanism: independent-across-constituents but heteroscedastic injection (per-node variances spanning 0.2–3.0) yields decisively non-uniform per-mode power (Red measured 1.60, 2.34, 1.17, 1.40, 2.21, 1.04, 1.07, 1.98) while the zero-mode fraction of total power remains exactly $1/N$. I verified this analytically (execution tooling was unavailable in this session, so the check is by closed form rather than by re-running Red's script): for independent zero-mean injection with diagonal covariance $D$ projected onto the orthonormal Laplacian eigenbasis $V$, per-mode power is $(V^\top D V)_{kk} = \sum_i V_{ik}^2 d_i$, generally non-uniform across $k$ unless $D \propto I$; the zero mode $v_0 = \mathbf 1/\sqrt N$ carries $\sum_i (1/N) d_i = \bar d/N$ against total $\operatorname{tr} D = N\bar d$, so its fraction is $1/N$ for any independent $D$. The $1/N$ is governed by independence (variance-of-the-mean), not by equipartition over eigenmodes. The prose pins a correct number on the wrong variable.

2. **Line 2651, first equality.** $\mathcal{I}_{s\to s+1}$ is defined at `:2233` (Eq. `cross_scale_information_flow`) as a sum of full transported Gaussian KLs $\sum_i \mathrm{KL}(q_i^{(s)} \| \Omega_{i,I}[q_I^{(s+1)}])$ with gauge transport $\Omega_{i,I} = \exp(\phi_i)\exp(-\phi_j)$. By the closed-form Gaussian KL (`external_canon_math.md:42`), this collapses to the pure-mean dispersion $\sum_i \tfrac12(\mu_i-\mu_I)^\top\Lambda(\mu_i-\mu_I)$ only when both $\Sigma_i=\Sigma_I$ and $\Omega_{i,I}=I$. The common-$\Sigma$ stipulation at `:2628` buys the first; nothing buys the second. Both teams executed the same check (transport-induced trace and log-det terms dominate and survive common-$\Sigma$), and Blue's rebuttal concedes the `=` is an overclaim and that no gauge-fixing convention forcing $\Omega_{i,I}=I$ exists in the framework (searched, line 1609 concerns a different construction). The disambiguation note's equality (i) is false as written.

3. **Line 2656, first sentence.** "Because the bit is reparametrization-invariant, it is a scale-invariant unit, and the slowdown cannot reside in a rescaling of the unit." The invariance grounded at `:2596` is Cencov coordinate-invariance on the belief manifold (`external_canon_math.md:24`, `:60`), not invariance under RG coarse-graining across scales $s\to s+1$. Coordinate-invariance of an object at fixed scale places no constraint on its image under the blocking map. Blue's rebuttal concedes this sentence is a non-sequitur.

## Reasoning

The claim is compound (00_claim.md components A, B, C), and the evidence partitions cleanly rather than favoring one team across the board, which is what makes REMAND the honest outcome here rather than an evasion. Three pieces are sound and survive both rounds. Component (B), $\mathrm{KL} = \tfrac12 ds^2$ at `:2593`, is the exact pure-mean equal-covariance specialization of the second-order Fisher expansion (`external_canon_math.md:35`, `:42`); both teams verified it to round-off and Red conceded it. The component (A) trace-ratio limit $Z_I \to 1/N$ is a theorem under the independence and common-$\Lambda$ assumptions stated at the point of use (`:2633`): $\operatorname{tr}(\Lambda v_\xi/N)/\operatorname{tr}(\Lambda v_\xi) = 1/N$ by linearity of the trace for any common $\Lambda$ and any $v_\xi$, requiring no isotropy. The second equality of Eq. `Ilow_eq_dispersion` at `:2651` is exact Parseval on the orthonormal node eigenbasis with $\Lambda$ acting on the feature index of the tensor product, needing no commutation of $\Lambda$ with $L$; Red verified it symbolically and withdrew its opening attack on it. Component (C)'s conclusion — $Z^{(s)}$ is a comoving-units choice, not a dynamical critical exponent — survives on its load-bearing second argument at `:2656`, which rests on the Hohenberg–Halperin definition of $z$ via $\tau\sim\xi^z$ at a critical point plus the manuscript's own open-fixed-point flag at `:2227`, and never invokes reparametrization-invariance. Red conceded this definitional point in rebuttal.

What does not survive as written are the three items above, each a defect of stated exactness or stated mechanism rather than of substance. The sharpest of these is the line 2649 prose, the one point Blue did not cleanly concede. Blue framed it as "a stated regime, not derived" — a milder defect of under-discharge. Reading the actual text settles it against Blue's framing: the clause "with power spread uniformly over all $N$ eigenmodes" is non-restrictive (comma-set-off, predicated of incoherent injection), and "which is the spectral origin of $Z_I = 1/N$" makes that uniformity the asserted cause. The manuscript is not silent on why the $1/N$ holds spectrally; it offers a specific mechanism, and that mechanism is false in the manuscript's own sense of incoherence (independence, `:2633`), since independence delivers variance-of-the-mean on the single zero mode, not equipartition over all modes. A correct number reached through a stated-but-false mechanism is not a rigorous formalization of that number. The author's equation-level discipline elsewhere in the same subsection (the deliberate `≈` at `:2644` and `:2640` versus the `=` at `:2651`) sets the standard the prose at `:2649` and the equality at `:2651` fall short of. The compound claim's verdict that the subsection "is a rigorous formalization" therefore holds for (B), the (A) trace-ratio, both equalities-as-corrected, and (C)'s conclusion, but fails for the (A) spectral mechanism as written and the two overclaimed exactness/justification statements. The remedy is three surgical line edits, not a reworking of the construction, which is precisely the situation REMAND with itemization is for.

## Action

Three manuscript edits, each surgical and none touching the physics or the surviving components.

Line 2649: replace the causal mechanism clause. The $1/N$ is the variance-of-the-mean under independence — the barycenter is the projection onto the constant zero mode $\mathbf 1/\sqrt N$ of $L$, and independent injection of any (not necessarily isotropic) per-constituent covariance gives the zero mode a $1/N$ share of total power. Remove the assertion that incoherent injection has uniform power over all $N$ eigenmodes as the stated reason; equipartition is the special isotropic case and is not what delivers the result.

Line 2651, first equality: change `=` to `≈` and state the condition (leading order in the post-transport mean difference, $\Omega_{i,I}\approx I$), inheriting the qualifier already carried at `:2642`. Soften "exactly" at `:2649`. The second equality (Parseval) is exact and stays as `=`.

Line 2656, first sentence: drop or repair the reparametrization-invariance-implies-no-renormalization step; it conflates Cencov coordinate-invariance on the belief manifold with RG invariance across scales. The load-bearing second argument (law-of-large-numbers ratio, no critical point, open fixed point at `:2227`) already carries the conclusion and should be left to do so.

After these three edits the claim's "rigorous formalization" billing is defensible across all three components. No further debate round is needed; this is a copy-edit pass against a verified itemization.
