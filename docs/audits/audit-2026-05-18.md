### 2. debugger (bugs + gradients + theory invariants)

#### In-place indexed assignment on autograd-attached tensor in full-cov sigma handoff
**Location:** `transformer/vfe/stack.py:110`
**Severity:** high
**Evidence:** `new_prior_sigma[..., diag_idx, diag_idx] = new_prior_sigma[..., diag_idx, diag_idx].clamp(min=1e-4, max=self.sigma_max)`
**Fix:** Out-of-place addition; latent under default `prior_handoff_sigma=0.0` but a live bug the moment full-covariance sigma handoff is enabled.

#### `variational_ffn.py` fallback `sqrt(d_h)` uses `irrep_dims[0]` for all heads
**Location:** `transformer/core/variational_ffn.py:1309-1310`
**Severity:** medium
**Evidence:** `_d_h_canon = self.irrep_dims[0] if self.irrep_dims else 1; _sqrt_dh = math.sqrt(max(_d_h_canon, 1))`. Block-diagonal path uses per-head `d_h`; fallback hard-codes the first.

#### Orphan-LR detector for `e_sigma_lr` is a false negative under default `norm_type='mahalnorm'`
**Location:** `transformer/vfe/config.py:388-399`
**Severity:** low
**Evidence:** `_sigma_lift_via_norm = self.norm_type in ('mahalnorm', 'centered_mahalnorm')` — default `mahalnorm` suppresses the warning by design; documentation gap, not a correctness bug.
**Fix:** Comment clarifying when the warning is meaningful.

#### `use_output_projection=True` breaks the sandwich invariant with no runtime guard
**Location:** `transformer/core/attention.py:1629`
**Severity:** medium
**Evidence:** `self.output_proj = nn.Linear(embed_dim, embed_dim, bias=False)`. W_O applies to `mu_agg` only; `sigma` is left in the original frame. `BlockConfig.__post_init__` warns about `use_equivariant_head_mixer + skip_attention` but never about this.

#### Bare `except Exception` swallows tokenizer decode errors and optimizer state restoration
**Location:** `transformer/training/experiment_runner.py:656` (and repeats at 1115, 1273, 1504-1670)
**Severity:** low
**Evidence:** `except Exception: seq_info = f"Step {step}, ..."` — corrupted checkpoint at line 1115 silently restarts training from scratch.
**Fix:** Replace silent broad excepts on non-visualization paths with `logger.error(...)` plus conditional re-raise.

### 6. vfe-codebase-auditor (math purity vs standard literature)

#### F1. Manuscript F has 5 terms; code assembles 2 (+ entropy)
**Location:** `transformer/vfe/e_step.py:166`, `transformer/core/variational_ffn.py:28-32`, `transformer/vfe/trainer.py:651-655`
**Severity:** high
**Standard reference:** Friston (2010); Blei, Kucukelbir, Jordan (2017).
**Deviation:** The γ-coupled model-coupling term `Σ γ_ij KL(s_i ‖ Ω_ij s_j) + τ·γ·log(γ/π^s)` and the λ_h hyper-prior term `λ_h KL(s‖h)` are absent. No γ-attention parameter anywhere; the trainer's own comment (`vfe/trainer.py:651-655`) confirms.
**Fix:** Either implement the missing terms or restate the F functional in the manuscript as the 3-term implemented form.

#### F2. Outer M-step minimizes CE + auxiliary scalar, not full F
**Location:** `transformer/vfe/model.py:185-222`
**Severity:** high
**Standard reference:** Dempster, Laird, Rubin (1977); Blei et al. (2017).
**Evidence:** `loss = ce_loss + 0.5*mass_phi*phi_norm_sq + sum(block.e_step._aux_hyperparam_loss)`. The `α·KL(q‖p)` and `Σ β·KL` terms exist only as gradients inside the E-step inner loop; they never appear as a backward-graph-visible scalar that the outer optimizer sees.
**Deviation:** This is structurally amortized inference, not variational EM.
**Fix:** Either add the converged-q F to the outer loss, or document the architecture as amortized inference through an unrolled E-step.

#### F3. Hierarchical cross-layer cascade is mean-only
**Location:** `transformer/vfe/stack.py:81-125`, `transformer/vfe/config.py:53-54`
**Severity:** major
**Standard reference:** Friston (2017); Parr, Pezzulo, Friston (2022).
**Evidence:** Defaults `prior_handoff_rho=1.0`, `prior_handoff_sigma=0.0`. At each layer boundary `μ_p^{ℓ+1}=μ_q^ℓ` (full mean handoff), `σ_p^{ℓ+1}=σ_embedding` (frozen), `φ_p^{ℓ+1}=φ_embedding` (frozen).
**Deviation:** Posterior variance and gauge frame are discarded — point-passing, not distributional handoff.
**Fix:** Document as a point-estimate approximation, or change the default to `prior_handoff_sigma=1.0` (codepath already exists).

#### F4. `τ·log N·Σβ` constant drop in `_update_phi` only
**Location:** `transformer/vfe/e_step.py:899, 1208` vs `454-457`
**Severity:** minor — internally consistent.
**Fix:** No change required.

#### F5. `decode_log_scale` adds a learnable temperature not in the documented `−KL/τ` form
**Location:** `transformer/vfe/prior_bank.py:121, 366-367`
**Severity:** low
**Evidence:** `logits = -0.5 * scale / tau * (combined + prior_bias)` with `scale = torch.exp(self.decode_log_scale.clamp(-3.0, 3.0))`.
**Deviation:** Decode is `-c·KL/τ` with learnable `c ∈ [0.05, 20]`; manuscript and `prior_bank.py:8, 302` claim `-KL/τ`.
**Fix:** Document in the manuscript decode equation, or constrain `c=1` and remove the parameter.

#### F6. Sigma natural-gradient comment ambiguity (REFUTED — comment-only)
**Location:** `transformer/core/vfe_gradients.py:1975-1981`
**Severity:** low (claim invalid — comment-only evidence)
**Verifier REFUTED.**
